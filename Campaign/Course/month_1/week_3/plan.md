## Strahd Persona Notes — The Gentleman
> Strahd's first appearance sets the entire campaign tone. He must be:
> - Polished, never threatening
> - Curious about the players as *individuals*
> - Clearly, genuinely obsessed with Ireena in a way that's unsettling without being crude
> - Never clumsy or mustache-twirling — he is a 400-year-old aristocrat who has won every game he's ever played
