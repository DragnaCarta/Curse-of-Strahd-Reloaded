## Opening Recap (Read Aloud)

The sky, when you can see it, is overcast and colorless. You are no longer in Daggerford. You are somewhere else entirely. Ahead: the road continues west."

## Key NPC Notes
- **Ismark:** Don't make him the party's sidekick. He's capable but grieving. He defers to the party because he's exhausted, not because he's weak.
- **Ireena:** Push back on any party member who tries to tell her what to do. She's not a quest objective.
- **Doru:** Play him with heartbreak, not menace. His tragedy is that he's *still in there*.

## Doru Decision Tracking
> Record the party's decision about Doru here — it has downstream effects:
- [ ] Destroyed (Father Donavich's arc = grief → acceptance)
- [ ] Cured (Doru may reappear as an ally in Act II or III)
- [ ] Left unresolved (becomes a ticking clock — Doru eventually escapes the church)
