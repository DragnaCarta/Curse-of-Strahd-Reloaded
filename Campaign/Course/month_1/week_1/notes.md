## Key Moments
- **Daggerford opening:** Druid spoke to the dog using Speak with Animals — dog transmitted pure terror, images of the house, "DON'T GO" on repeat, then bolted east. Great early tension.
- **Rose & Thorn introduction:** Sorcerer rolled Charisma 10 on Rose — got basic info. Rose shared what she knew but didn't open up fully.
- **Paladin Divine Sense (Floor 1):** Mike used Divine Sense — detected Rose and Thorn as undead, and desecrated ground beneath the house. Rose didn't understand why he looked at her that way. Powerful moment.
- **Paladin held up his trinket:** Rose recognized it — said her mother wore something similar, an amber pendant. Breadcrumb to the shard planted.
- **Monk Perception 22 (Floor 1):** Found that Rose and Thorn leave no footprints, oil lamps have no fuel source. Established the Monk as the party's early warning system.
- **Rogue opened nursemaid's door (Floor 2):** Ben went in alone despite Sorcerer wanting to stay together. Got clawed and paralyzed by the ghoulified nursemaid. Party rushed in to save him.
- **Paladin crit with maul (Floor 2):** Mike rolled a natural 20 on the nursemaid. 12 damage. Table went wild.
- **Monk flying knee kill:** Finished the nursemaid mimic with 23 damage. Overkill by 13. The body dissolved into black ichor — no corpse left. Only the locket remained on the floor.
- **Ben paralyzed for multiple rounds:** Failed CON saves repeatedly (rolled a 1, then a 5). Eventually wore off after combat ended. Ben was bummed — give him a good moment next session.
- **Monk Investigation 23 (Floor 2 — Master Bedroom):** Jake investigated the master bedroom and found EVERYTHING — the vanity letter ("The nursemaid's child will not bear a name"), Rose's picture book ("Baby Walter cries all night"), and Gustav's fireplace carving ("SHE FED HIM TO IT"). Cracked the entire mystery wide open.
- **NOTE:** The full ghostly vision of Gustav and Elisabeth arguing was NOT triggered this session. The party has not yet witnessed it as a group. It can still play when the full party enters the master bedroom, or save it — Jake's investigation covered the key story beats already.
- **Rose remembers Walter:** After hearing the name, Rose recalled Elisabeth using the pendant and the name "Walter" to control the thing in the basement.
- **Ben picked up the nursemaid's doll:** Found a wooden disc inside — carved with the name WALTER and a sacrificial symbol.

## NPC Interactions
- **Rose:** Composed, helpful, led the party through the house. Remembered Walter's name when prompted. Growing trust with the party.
- **Thorn:** Mostly silent. Key line when asked about the trinket: "It smells like the basement." One player held his hand — he said "You're warm." 
- **Miss Klara (dark mimic):** Fought as a ghoulish dark construct — NOT the real Klara. The house's magic created a twisted guardian from Klara's suffering, bound to guard the nursery forever. When killed, the body **dissolved into black ichor** and seeped into the floorboards. Only the locket remained. Party learned her identity from Rose after the kill. **The real Klara's corpse is in a trunk on Floor 4 — they haven't found it yet.**
