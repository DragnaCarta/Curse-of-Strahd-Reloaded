# Week 1 — Session Recap

> Session 1 of 2 for Week 1. Death House is not complete — session ended mid-dungeon.

## What Happened (Summary)
The party met at The Nightmare's Bridle tavern in Daggerford during the Highharvesttide festival. Fog rolled in, trinkets pulled them east, and two ghost children — Rose and Thorn — led them to Death House (16 Durst Lane). The party explored Floors 1 and 2: fought animated wolves in the den, found Gustav's letter behind a hidden bookcase, survived a ghoulified nursemaid who paralyzed Ben the Rogue, discovered Elisabeth's amber shard pendant and an iron key in the master bedroom, and pieced together the story of Baby Walter through Jake's investigation of the room — the nursemaid's child who was sacrificed to the thing in the basement. The party leveled up to Level 2 after clearing Floor 2. Session ended with the party preparing to ascend to Floor 3.

## Key Decisions Made
- The Druid chose to speak to the dog — established early that something is deeply wrong
- The Paladin used Divine Sense on Floor 1 — knows Rose and Thorn are undead, knows the basement is desecrated
- Ben opened the nursemaid's door against the Sorcerer's advice to stay together — got paralyzed for it
- The Monk investigated the master bedroom thoroughly — uncovered the Walter/Elisabeth/Klara story
- The party has the amber shard and knows Walter's name can control the Flesh Mound

## Character Moments
- **Mike (Paladin):** Crit on the nursemaid with a nat 20 maul strike. Used Divine Sense to detect the children as undead — showed restraint by not attacking them.
- **Ben (Rogue):** Got paralyzed by the nursemaid — rough first combat. Found Walter's name disc inside the doll. Needs a hero moment next session.
- **Jake (Monk):** Monster perception and investigation rolls (22 and 23). Cracked the entire Durst family mystery. Held his trinket up to the amber shard.
- **Sorcerer:** Tried to keep the party together. Rolled Charisma on Rose for information. Needs more spotlight in the basement.
- **Druid:** Spoke with the dog (standout opening moment). Needs combat spotlight in the Flesh Mound fight.

## Plot Threads Opened
- The Flesh Mound is in the basement — the party knows it exists and has tools to fight it
- Rose and Thorn are ghosts who don't know they're dead — the toy chest reveal on Floor 3 is still ahead
- Elisabeth's amber shard controls the Mound when paired with the name "Walter"
- Gustav knew about the sacrifice ("SHE FED HIM TO IT")
- Miss Klara was more than a nursemaid — the locket with baby hair suggests she was Walter's mother

## Plot Threads Resolved
- Identity of the nursemaid: Miss Klara, killed in combat
- The family portrait mystery: confirmed Rose and Thorn haven't aged — they're ghosts
- The amber pendant from the vision: found and taken

## Where We Left Off
**Floor 2, Death House.** Party has just leveled to Level 2. All rooms on Floors 1–2 explored. Rose is leading them toward Floor 3 to show them "our room." The toy chest reveal, Floor 4 attic, basement, cult shrine, and Flesh Mound boss fight are all ahead.

**Party condition:** Everyone at full Level 2 HP except Ben (took claw damage from nursemaid — confirm remaining HP at start of next session).
