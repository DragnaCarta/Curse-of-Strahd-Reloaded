## Atmosphere Notes
- Ambient music suggestion: dark orchestral, children's music box tones, silence
- Fog machine / low lighting recommended at table if playing in person
- Death House is horror — calibrate to your table's comfort level (Session Zero topics: body horror, childhood death)

## Contingency Plans
- If party refuses to enter Death House: the fog prevents departure and the trinkets grow painfully hot
- If a PC dies: they are absorbed by the Flesh Mound; recoverable as described in plot.md
- If party burns the house down: it reforms overnight — the curse rebuilds it
