## Opening — How to Start

1. Start the Toccata before anyone sits down
2. Let it play 20–30 seconds while everyone settles
3. Speak over the quieter middle section:
   > *"The fog has not lifted. It never does. Welcome back to Barovia."*
   
   *(Yes — say this even on Session 1. It's your ritual. It starts now.)*
4. Then immediately into the Daggerford read-aloud from `plot.md`

---

## Daggerford (15–20 Minutes — Then Cut It)

**What it is:** Highharvesttide — a harvest festival. Torches, food stalls, music, drinking. The **Nightmare's Bridle** is the tavern. This is the last normal moment of the campaign. Make it feel safe.

**What to offer:**
- Drinks, introductions between characters, one round of banter
- The festival outside — describe the warmth, the noise, the smell of roasting meat
- One atmospheric detail: a dog at the edge of the square staring east, refusing to move

**Cut it the moment anyone tries to go deep:**
> *"As you turn toward the smithy, the lanterns along the street flicker all at once. The music stops mid-note. And from somewhere in the thickening fog — a child's voice."*

They never come back to Daggerford. Every minute there is borrowed time.

## Rose & Thorn — Getting Them Into the House

**Playing Rose:** Composed, precise, disturbingly patient. She doesn't beg — she reasons. Too still for a child standing in the rain.

**Playing Thorn:** Says almost nothing. When he speaks it is one sentence, completely flat, and completely true.

**If the party hesitates — escalate in order, stop when one works:**

1. The fog presses in — moving away from the house puts them in zero-visibility fog. The house is the only solid thing in the world.
2. Rose starts crying — not asking, not pleading. Quietly. The way a child cries when they've stopped expecting help.
3. Something growls in the fog — close, large. The house door swings open. Warm light, smell of something cooking.
4. *(Preferred)* Rose takes the most empathetic player's hand: *"You're the first people who have ever come. Please don't leave us alone."*
5. *(Nuclear — save it)* Thorn, looking at the ground: *"The house won't let us leave. But it will let you in."*

## If Someone Fights Rose or Thorn

CR 4 ghosts vs Level 1 party = near-TPK. **Session 1 rule: nobody dies permanently.**

When the second character drops to 0 HP, Rose stops. She looks at the bodies.
> *"I just — I wasn't trying to — "*

Thorn: *"They're frightened. That's all."*

The downed characters wake with 1 HP. Rose and Thorn step into the walls and are gone. The party is now alone in the house with no guides. They earned that. Let it be uncomfortable.

> Full combat rules: `Campaign/Course/rules/death_and_resurrection.md`

## If Someone Actually Dies (Sessions 2+ Rule — Not Session 1)

Session 1 is protected. After Session 1:
1. Describe the death cinematically
2. Ask the player: spirit or new character?
3. **Spirit:** Copy `Campaign/Course/templates/spirit_character.md` → save as `spirit_[name].md` in this session's week folder
4. **New character:** Enters at full party level within 15 minutes

## Contingency Plans

| Situation | Response |
|-----------|---------|
| Party won't enter the house | Fog tightens, trinkets grow hot, escalate hooks above |
| Party tries to burn the house | It reforms overnight. The curse rebuilds it. |
| Party flees the Flesh Mound | Shadows pursue 2 rooms. House collapses in 10 min/floor. |
| PC hits 0 HP (Session 1) | Unconscious — wakes with 1 HP after threat passes. Dark Powers tasting them. |
| Party skips the basement entirely | The house locks all exits until the ritual chamber is approached |
