## The Sunsword — Rules Summary
- Blade of sunlight, 15-foot radius bright (sunlight for vampire mechanics)
- +2d8 radiant bonus damage against undead
- Strahd's regeneration does NOT function in its light
- The wielder can suppress the light as a bonus action
