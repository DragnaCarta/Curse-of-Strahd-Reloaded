2. What did Patrina do? → Offered to help Strahd gain immortality; stoned by her own people

4. Does he know the Sunsword is linked to Patrina? → Not yet. He will find out at the Temple. This will break him.

## Elder Burebis — Death Scene
> His death should be gentle and peaceful, not dramatic.
> He has been waiting to pass on this knowledge. Having done so, he is ready.
> The mountain folk grieve with dignity.
> Do not skip this — it humanizes the lore and gives it emotional weight.
