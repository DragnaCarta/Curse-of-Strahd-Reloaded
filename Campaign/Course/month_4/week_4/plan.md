## The Dark Powers' Bargain — Key Rules
> If ANY player says yes to the bargain: the Dark Powers take Ireena immediately.
> Strahd is put to sleep, the fog lifts, but Ireena is gone forever.
> This is a valid ending — dark but valid. It's the "tragic victory" ending.
> Prepare for both outcomes.

## The Dawn Scene — Maximum Impact
- Describe the light in physical, sensory terms (warmth, smell, the feel of actual sun on skin)
- Go around the table: "The first thing your character does when the sun comes out is —"
- Don't rush the epilogues. This is the payoff for 4 months of work.
