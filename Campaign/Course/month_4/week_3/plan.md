## Baba Lysaga Fight — Priority Sequence
1. Party should target the skull first (forces her to the ground)
2. Once grounded, her regeneration is the main challenge
3. If party consecrates the earth before she dies, regeneration stops
4. She dissolves at 0 HP — not a physical body left behind

## The Karl Hostage — Resolution Timing
> This should NOT bog down the session. It is a side problem to solve creatively:
> - Best outcome: Keepers of the Feather extract him (Muriel / Urwin)
> - Fallback: party goes themselves (but this delays the Fane)
> - Worst case: Karl is taken to Ravenloft (becomes another heist objective in Week 4)

## Heart of Sorrow Reveal
> This is the campaign's penultimate crisis.
> The visual: the red pulse wave, then total darkness spreading over Barovia
> The auditory: silence first, then the sound of thousands of undead beginning to move
> The emotional: all the work the party has done — and it still wasn't enough to stop THIS
> The reframe: they still have the Sunsword. They still have their allies. This is survivable.
