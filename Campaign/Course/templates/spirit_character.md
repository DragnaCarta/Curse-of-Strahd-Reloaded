# Spirit Character Sheet
> **When a character dies:**
> 1. Copy this file
> 2. Save it to the **current session's week folder** alongside the other session files:
>    `Campaign/Course/month_X/week_Y/spirit_[character_name].md`
>    *(Example: a character dies in Month 2, Week 3 → save as `Campaign/Course/month_2/week_3/spirit_aldric.md`)*
> 3. Fill in the Identity and Tether sections with the player before the session ends
> 4. From that point forward, this file lives in that week folder permanently as the record of where and when they died
>
> The player keeps playing — just differently. They level up with the party. They have a quest to get back.

## Spirit Stats

The spirit retains all of their character's mental stats, personality, knowledge, and class features that do not require a physical body. Physical stats are replaced.

| Stat | Value | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| STR | — | No physical form |
| DEX | — | No physical form |
| CON | — | No physical form |
| INT | Keep character's INT | Full mental capacity |
| WIS | Keep character's WIS | Full perception |
| CHA | Keep character's CHA | Voice and presence intact |

**Spirit HP:** Equal to the character's level × 4  
**AC:** 11 + WIS modifier (incorporeal deflection)  
**Speed:** 30 ft. fly (hover) — passes through solid objects  
**Condition Immunities:** Exhaustion, Grappled, Paralyzed, Petrified, Poisoned, Prone, Restrained  
**Damage Immunities:** Poison, Bludgeoning/Piercing/Slashing from nonmagical weapons  
**Damage Resistances:** All other damage except Force, Radiant, and Necrotic

## Spirit Abilities

### What They Can Do

**Communicate (always):**
Speak normally with anyone who can hear them. Visible as a translucent version of themselves in dim light or darkness. In bright light: only a faint shimmer and a voice.

**Possess (Willing Only — Action):**
Enter a willing party member's body. The host retains full control of all actions. The spirit can:
- Whisper suggestions (the host hears them as intrusive thoughts)
- Share skill proficiencies with the host for the duration
- Use **one** class feature per possession that doesn't require a physical form (e.g., Bardic Inspiration, spells with no material components, Channel Divinity if flavor allows)

Possession ends when the host chooses, when the spirit chooses, or when the host drops to 0 HP.
**Limit:** Once per short rest.

**Distract (Action):**
Scream directly into one creature's mind within 30 ft.
Target makes DC 12 Wisdom save or has **disadvantage on their next attack roll or saving throw**.
This is invisible to other creatures — they just see their ally suddenly hesitate.

**Haunt Location (1 hour ritual):**
The spirit bonds to a specific room or 30-ft area. For 24 hours:
- The spirit knows exactly who enters and what they do
- Can manipulate one small object per hour (open a door, move a candle, drop a key)
- Any creature sleeping in the area must make DC 12 Wisdom save or have disturbing dreams (no mechanical effect — just atmosphere)

**Communicate With Animals / Beasts:**
Spirits in Barovia are perceptible to animals. Beasts will not attack the spirit and will carry simple messages (one concept, not words) to a target within 1 mile.

---

### What They Cannot Do

- Cannot make attack rolls with weapons
- Cannot cast spells with material or somatic components (verbal-only spells still work)
- Cannot carry, move, or interact with objects weighing more than 1 lb without the Haunt Location ritual
- Cannot leave Barovia (this was already true when alive)
- Cannot be healed by healing spells (use Spirit HP recovery below)

---

## Spirit HP Recovery

**Short Rest:** Recover Spirit HP equal to 1d6 + WIS modifier  
**Long Rest:** Recover all Spirit HP  
**Special:** When the tether is actively worked toward (the party takes a meaningful action toward resolving the unfinished business), the spirit recovers 2d6 Spirit HP immediately — the soul strengthens when it has purpose.

---

## Leveling Up as a Spirit

The spirit levels up with the party at every milestone. They gain:
- All class features as normal
- New spell slots (verbal-only spells still usable)
- Increased Spirit HP (recalculate as level × 4)
- All mental stat improvements (INT, WIS, CHA ASIs)
- Physical stat improvements go onto their **Living Character Sheet** — applied when they return to their body

> **The living character sheet is not abandoned.** Keep it updated alongside this sheet. When they return, they return at full party level with everything they earned.

## Returning to Life

### The Requirements (ALL must be met)

- [ ] **The body exists** and has not been burned or claimed by Strahd
- [ ] **The tether is resolved** — the unfinished business is complete
- [ ] **A return method is available** (see below)

### Return Methods

| Method | When Available | Cost |
|--------|---------------|------|
| **The Abbot's Grace** | Month 3, Week 4 (post-redemption) | The Abbot spends 3 days in prayer; available once per spirit; he will not explain why he is limited |
| **The Amber Temple ritual** | Month 4, Week 2 | Exethanter knows a ritual using the summoning circle; requires a diamond worth 500 gp (found in the temple) and the spirit's willing consent |
| **Sergei's Intercession** | Month 4, Week 2 (after Sunsword empowerment) | Sergei can petition the Morninglord for one soul — only usable if the spirit never accepted a Dark Gift |
| **The Ladies Three** | If all three Fanes are consecrated | As the land heals, one spirit may be returned as a gift of the restored Fanes — chosen by the DM |

### The Return Scene

When a spirit returns to their body:

> *"You fall into yourself like a stone into water. Everything is loud — your own heartbeat is deafening. The cold is gone. You feel heavy in a way you had forgotten weight could feel. You breathe. It hurts. It is the best pain you have ever felt."*

They wake with 1 HP. They gain one level of Exhaustion (removed after a long rest). Their physical stats are exactly as the living character sheet showed — every level gained as a spirit counts. They are themselves again, fully. Just changed.

---

## The Dark Alternative — Fading

If the tether is never resolved and no return method is found, the spirit fades.

**The Fade Timeline:**
- **Sessions 1–3 after death:** Full coherence. Normal spirit play.
- **Sessions 4–5:** Occasional confusion. Forgets minor details. Repeats a phrase sometimes without noticing.
- **Session 6:** The player describes what their character most wanted to say to someone in the party but never did. The spirit says it. Then they are gone.

The Dark Powers have them now. Quietly. Finally.

**This should feel like a send-off, not a punishment.** It is one of the most powerful scenes you can run. The player gets a meaningful exit. The party carries the loss forward.
