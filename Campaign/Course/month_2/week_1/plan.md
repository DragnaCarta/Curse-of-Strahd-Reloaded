## Volenta Fight Notes
- She's fun, not scary. Play her like a chaotic child who happens to be a murderous vampire.
- She ESCAPES at 0 HP — she is not killed here. She reappears at Castle Ravenloft.
- The vampire spawn are tragic — point out the cobbler's apron and baker's hat. Give them humanity even as enemies.

## Father Lucian vs. Volenta Contrast
- Father Lucian: warm, real, genuinely good — make the players *like* him
- Volenta: bright, manic, genuinely evil — make the players *hate* her
- The contrast is the arc's emotional core: good things are worth protecting
