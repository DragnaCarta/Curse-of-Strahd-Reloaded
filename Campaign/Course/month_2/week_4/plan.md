## Leo Dilisnya Background (for flavor during fight)
> 300 years ago, Leo Dilisnya served the Order of the Silver Dragon. He betrayed them to Strahd — opening a gate that allowed Strahd's forces into Argynvostholt. He felt no remorse. He was executed and his soul refused to pass on, trapped in guilt and pride. He feeds on weaker spirits in the Ethereal Plane. He is what happens when a person never admits they were wrong.

## Stella Wachter Rescue — Emotional Beats

- Victor looks at Stella from across the room and says nothing. His feelings are obvious.

## Baba Lysaga Tone
- She's not a hag in the traditional sense — she's more like a tragic figure who has completely lost her mind
- She's polite to guests (hospitality is old magic) — but erratic and slightly wrong
- She should feel POWERFUL even while being helpful. The party should understand: this is not an enemy to make today.
