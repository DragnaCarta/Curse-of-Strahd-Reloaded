- [ ] **The Cult of Amber = precursor to the Amber Temple.** Elisabeth's amber shard is a fragment of an amber sarcophagus from the temple. Each shard has a psychic link to a specific vestige (dark power) imprisoned there. Strahd himself bargained with the vestiges to gain his vampiric powers.
