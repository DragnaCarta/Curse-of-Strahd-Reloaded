# Rolling Stats — Quick Reference for New Players

## The Method: 4d6 Drop Lowest

You need **four six-sided dice** (4d6). If you only have one, roll it four times.

### Steps

1. **Roll 4d6.** Add up the three highest. Write down the total. Ignore the lowest die.
2. **Do that 6 times.** You should have 6 numbers.
3. **Assign each number to one stat** — your choice which number goes where.

### Example

| Roll | Dice Rolled | Drop Lowest | Total |
|------|-------------|-------------|-------|
| 1 | 4, 3, 5, 1 | drop the 1 | **12** |
| 2 | 6, 6, 4, 2 | drop the 2 | **16** |
| 3 | 3, 2, 3, 5 | drop the 2 | **11** |
| 4 | 5, 4, 4, 1 | drop the 1 | **13** |
| 5 | 6, 3, 5, 4 | drop the 3 | **15** |
| 6 | 2, 1, 4, 3 | drop the 1 | **9** |

Results: **16, 15, 13, 12, 11, 9** — now assign them.

## The Six Stats (What They Do)

| Stat | Short Version | Put Your Highest Here If... |
|------|--------------|---------------------------|
| **Strength (STR)** | Hit hard, carry stuff, break doors | You want to hit things with a sword or axe |
| **Dexterity (DEX)** | Dodge, sneak, shoot, go first | You want to be fast, stealthy, or use a bow |
| **Constitution (CON)** | How much damage you survive | **Everyone** wants this to be decent — it's your HP |
| **Intelligence (INT)** | Book smarts, magic knowledge | You're a Wizard |
| **Wisdom (WIS)** | Street smarts, awareness, faith | You're a Cleric, Druid, or Ranger |
| **Charisma (CHA)** | Persuasion, deception, force of personality | You're a Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, or Paladin |

---

## Where to Put Them — By Class

| Class | #1 Priority | #2 Priority | Don't Dump |
|-------|------------|------------|-----------|
| **Barbarian** | STR | CON | DEX |
| **Bard** | CHA | DEX | CON |
| **Cleric** | WIS | CON | STR or DEX |
| **Druid** | WIS | CON | DEX |
| **Fighter (melee)** | STR | CON | DEX |
| **Fighter (ranged)** | DEX | CON | STR |
| **Monk** | DEX | WIS | CON |
| **Paladin** | STR | CHA | CON |
| **Ranger** | DEX | WIS | CON |
| **Rogue** | DEX | CON | CHA or WIS |
| **Sorcerer** | CHA | CON | DEX |
| **Warlock** | CHA | CON | DEX |
| **Wizard** | INT | CON | DEX |

**Rule of thumb:** Your main stat goes highest, CON goes second or third (it keeps you alive), and your lowest number goes in whatever stat your class never uses.

---

## Reroll Floor (Optional — DM's Call)

If a player's six stats total less than **70**, or if no single stat is **14 or higher**, let them reroll all six. First-time players should feel capable, not crippled.

---

## After Rolling

1. Write each stat number next to the stat name on your character sheet
2. Your class and race may add bonuses — apply those after
3. The **modifier** is what you actually add to rolls:

| Stat Score | Modifier |
|-----------|----------|
| 8–9 | -1 |
| 10–11 | +0 |
| 12–13 | +1 |
| 14–15 | +2 |
| 16–17 | +3 |
| 18–19 | +4 |
| 20 | +5 |

That's it. Roll, assign, play.
