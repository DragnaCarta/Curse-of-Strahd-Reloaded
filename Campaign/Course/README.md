**Duration:** 4 months | **Sessions:** 16 weekly sessions (3–4 hours each) | **Levels:** 1 → 17  
**Milestone Leveling:** 1–2 levels per session | **System:** D&D 5th Edition  
**Source Material:** DragnaCarta's *Curse of Strahd: Reloaded* (this repository)

## Campaign Overview

The party is drawn into the demiplane of Barovia — a fog-shrouded valley ruled by the vampire **Strahd von Zarovich**, who seeks to escape his prison through a ritual called the **Grand Conjunction**. To stop him, the players must build alliances, gather three sacred artifacts, reconsecrate the valley's ancient Fanes, and storm Castle Ravenloft for a final confrontation.

## Critical Setup — BEFORE SESSION 1
Before the first session, **determine Madam Eva's Tarokka draws** and record them in:
- `month_1/week_1/plan.md` (permanent record)
- `month_1/week_3/plan.md` (used during the reading)
- `month_1/week_3/recap.md` (finalized record)

The Tarokka draws determine the locations of:
1. The **Tome of Strahd**
2. The **Holy Symbol of Ravenkind**
3. The **Sunsword** hilt
4. The **Foretold Ally**

These locations must be consistent throughout the campaign. Set them once and never change them.

## The Three Fanes (Act IV Objective)

| Fane | Location | What's Needed | Guardian |
|------|----------|---------------|---------|
| Forest Fane | Yester Hill | Spear of the Huntress (party collects Month 3) | N/A after druids cleared |
| Swamp Fane | Berez | Weaver's Gem (Martikovs carry it) | Baba Lysaga (boss) |
| Mountain Fane | Mt. Ghakis base | Seeker's Gem (Martikovs carry it) | Strahd's hostage crisis |

**Effect of reconsecrating all three:** Strahd loses all lair actions and land-based regeneration in the final battle.
