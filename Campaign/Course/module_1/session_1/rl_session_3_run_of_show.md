## Phase 5 — Storage Room (Center): Klara's Body, Specter Chase (20 min)

- Sheet-covered shapes, old iron stove. Players investigating find the **trunk** half-hidden under a moth-eaten curtain against the back wall.
- **Read aloud `plot.md` line 217–223** when they open the trunk:
> *"Inside — a woman's body. Real. Not a ghost. Skin tight over bone. Wrists scarred. Fingers removed. Eyes sewn shut. Lips and teeth torn out. She starved to death in this trunk."*
- Hold the silence. *"You killed a shadow puppet on Floor 3. The real woman has been in this trunk for three hundred years."*
- **DC 14 Wisdom (Medicine):** confirms starvation, weeks of torture before death.
- **The moment anyone touches the body:** Klara's specter erupts.
- **Specter stat block:** AC 12, HP 22, +4 Life Drain (3d6 necrotic, max HP reduction). She phases through walls/floors. **She pursues — does not stay in the storage room.**
- **Recommend the party flee** rather than fight. The chase IS the encounter.
- **Hidden door behind the trunk:** DC 14 Investigation. If Rose is possessing a player, she points it out: *"That's the way down. That's where they did the bad things."*

**Cliffhang option:** if you're at hour 2:30+ and the party is still finding the door, just have Rose point it out. Don't gate this.

## Phase 6 — Spiral Stairs (5 min)

- Door swings open onto a narrow stone staircase descending into absolute darkness — **three full floors down.**
- Party descends. Klara screams behind them.
- At the bottom: she hits an invisible barrier at the threshold, **dissolves into a wail of grief.** Cult magic repels her.
- The chanting begins below. *"One must die. One must die. ONE MUST DIE."*

**This is your safe cliffhang point #1.** If you're out of time at 2:00–2:30, end the session here. Roll initiative for next time.

## Phase 7 — Cult Initiates' Quarters (15 min)

- Stone corridor, dorms with rotting bunks, scrawled cult prayers on the walls in dried blood.
- **Drasha's journal** in a footlocker (footlocker 25E per source).
- Names. Descriptions. Each entry ends ***"Fed to Walter."***
- **THIS IS THE TRUTH-REVEAL MOMENT.** Let the party piece it together. Don't lecture.
- Rose and Thorn watch them read it. Thorn: *"Walter is our brother. Mommy hurt him."* Rose: *"Mommy hurt all of us."*

**If players don't connect it:** prompt with *"Who's Walter? Have you seen that name before?"* — they may remember the family portrait plaque from Floor 1.

**Skip if behind:** the well/cultist quarters in Phase 8 — go straight from this room to the Ritual Chamber.

## Phase 9 — Ritual Chamber: Flesh Mound (40 min)

- Dark cave-chamber. 13 cult-shadows around an altar. Chanting *"One must die!"*
- On the altar: a thing of meat and bone the size of a horse, breathing slowly. Asleep.
- **Three resolution paths:**
  1. **Sacrifice a living creature** → mound eats it. Does NOT free the party. (Use as horror moment if a player suggests it; the cult's curse persists.)
  2. **Attack the mound** → it screeches awake. Full combat. **Stats:** HP 68 / AC 15 / multiattack 3 tendrils +5 (2d6+3 bludgeoning).
  3. **Use amber shard to command "Walter"** → DC 13 CHA Intimidation (Jake or whoever holds the shard). On success: mound recognizes its name, can be peacefully released. **This is the campaign's preferred resolution.**
- **Combat tactics tip:** the depleted variant is L3-appropriate for 4 PCs. Don't add reinforcements.
- **If Jake drops to 0 HP at any point:** the **shard whispers.** Dark silhouette appears in his vision. *"I can save you. Take my hand."* No price named. If accepted: full HP restored, advance to Stage 2 corruption. (See `Campaign/Items/quest_items/elisabeth_amber_shard.md` and source `Amber Shards.md`.)

**FALLBACK CLIFFHANG (if at 2:30+):** end the session BEFORE rolling initiative on the mound. Read: *"The chant rises. The thing on the altar opens one yellow eye and sees you. Roll initiative — next session."*

**Behind them: the house collapses into a black pit.**

Decide at table: the shard *appears* to crumble but reforms intact in Jake's pocket, the Dark Powers having claimed him. That's a much better long-term hook.)
