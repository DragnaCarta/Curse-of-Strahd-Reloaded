## What Happened (Summary)
The party met at The Nightmare's Bridle tavern in Daggerford during the Highharvesttide festival. Fog rolled in, trinkets pulled them east, and two ghost children — Rose and Thorn — led them to Death House (16 Durst Lane). **They did not fight the animated wolves in the den** (never triggered that encounter). Floor 1: among other exploration, **Gustav's letter** behind a hidden bookcase. Floor 2: **dark mimic of the nursemaid** (Klara's torment given form — dissolved to ichor; not her real remains) paralyzed Ben the Rogue; Paladin crit; Monk finished the fight. **Elisabeth's amber shard** and **iron key** in the master bedroom; Jake's investigation uncovered Walter / Elisabeth / Klara story; Ben found **Walter's name disc** in the doll. Level 2 after Floor 2. Session ended preparing to ascend to Floor 3. **Lancelot** (Daggerford dog) and Floor 2 death are **Session 2 cold-open canon** — see Session 2 section above.

## Key Decisions Made
- The Druid chose to speak to the dog — established early that something is deeply wrong
- The Paladin used Divine Sense on Floor 1 — knows Rose and Thorn are undead, knows the basement is desecrated
- Ben opened the nursemaid's door against the Sorcerer's advice to stay together — got paralyzed for it
- The Monk investigated the master bedroom thoroughly — uncovered the Walter/Elisabeth/Klara story
- The party has the amber shard and knows Walter's name can control the Flesh Mound
