## 1. Floor 3 landing — armor fight

- Read-aloud + **four cuirassiers** when someone steps onto the balcony (`plot.md`).
- **When the last knight falls:** House shudders; harpsichord **stops** three heartbeats, then resumes **wrong key, slower**.  
  **Transition line you can use:** *"The metal stops ringing. Your ears are full of your own breathing. To one side: shelves and a desk — the library. To the other: that door, and the harpsichord stumbling through the same broken note. The stairwell gapes behind you. What do you do?"*

## 2. After the armors — two doors (player choice)

They can tackle **Library** and **Conservatory** in either order. Consequences:

| Order | What happens |
|--------|----------------|
| **Library first** (cautious) | They find **Klara’s resignation letter** (desk). Bookshelf: **trap** if someone **pulls the switch** without thinking — dart volley (`plot.md`). Safer approach: find the **niche**, use **amber shard** — opens **secret room** → skeleton, **Strahd’s letter**, first time they see his name. |
| **Conservatory first** (bold / curious) | **Harpsichord**, *Waltz for Klara*. Playing triggers vision + **Elisabeth’s Wrath** (DC 14 WIS, psychic, can kill). **On success or failure:** `plot.md` says the **library bookshelf scrapes open** — secret route becomes available *without* them needing to puzzle the niche first (the house is showing off). They should still **read Strahd’s letter** when they explore that room. |

**DM note:** If they **already** opened the secret room with the shard, Elisabeth’s event is still scary but the “bookshelf opens” beat is redundant — skip duplicate noise, maybe *“the house shudders; dust falls from the shelf you already opened.”*

## 3. Floor 3 — what they should leave with

- [ ] **Strahd von Zarovich** name seen (letter) — they don’t know who he is yet  
- [ ] **Klara** context deepened (library letter + later attic)  
- [ ] **Shard** used at least once if they went through the niche (you track charges only if you want; by default the shard is not consumed)  
- [ ] Harpsichord / Elisabeth beat **if** someone interacts with the instrument

## 4. Up to Floor 4 (attic)

- Find **stairs to attic** (from Reloaded / your house map — usually off the third floor; if unsure, *a narrow servants’ stair behind a door on this floor*).
- **Read-aloud** attic arrival (`plot.md`): mold, skittering, **unstable floors** (heavy armor or **running** → DC 12 DEX or fall to Floor 3, 1d6).

**Room order (suggested):**

1. **Children’s room** — **centipede swarm** in toy chest **before** skeletons; then **Rose & Thorn** revelation; optional **Rose possession**.  
2. **Spare bedroom** — music box, **basement map** (QUARTERS / SHRINE / ALTAR).  
3. **Storage** — **real Klara** in trunk → **specter** chases until basement stair threshold.

---

## 5. Into the basement

- **Hidden door** in storage (DC 14 Investigation, or **Rose** if possessed: *“That’s the way down.”*).  
- **Read-aloud** spiral stair (`plot.md`).  
- **Scene 3–5** in `plot.md`: cult shrine, **Flesh Mound**, sacrifice / fight / flee, **collapse**, **Level 3** when they **escape the house**.

---

## 6. Pacing — one session vs two

| If you have ~3 hours | Likely stop |
|----------------------|------------|
| **~2 hours left** after armors | End on **Floor 3** cliffhanger (library secret found OR Elisabeth scream), or on **attic stairs** with **centipede** tease next time. |
| **~4+ hours** | Push through **attic + specter + basement + Mound + escape** in one night (heavy). |

**Good Session 2 endpoint:** They’ve cleared Floor 3, done **one** of library/conservatory deeply, and are on the **attic landing** (centipedes next) — or they’ve **opened the basement stair** and you **fade to black** on the first line of chanting.
