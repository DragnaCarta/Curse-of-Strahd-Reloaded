## Vertical structure (top to bottom)

```
Floor 4 (Attic)         ← horror beats + basement entry
    ↑ secret stairs (behind Floor 3 Library bookshelf)
Floor 3                 ← party currently here (Library)
Floor 2                 ← bedrooms, master suite (amber shard origin)
Floor 1                 ← main hall, front door, dining
    ↓ spiral stairs (hidden behind trunk in Floor 4 Storage Room)
Basement                ← Cult of Amber lair, Flesh Mound
```

The party never descends through Floors 3→2→1 to reach the basement. They go **up to Floor 4, then plunge straight down via the spiral staircase** behind Klara's trunk.

### Recommended Floor 4 visit order (emotional pacing)
1. Children's Room (sad — Rose realizes she's dead)
2. Spare Bedroom (eerie — Strahd's name, basement map)
3. Storage Room (terror — Klara's body, specter chase, basement entry)
- *(Optional 0 or 4: Nursemaid's Suite — gentle grief beat with mirror Klara, only if pacing allows.)*

### Floor 4 hazards to remember
- **Rotten floors:** anyone in heavy armor or moving more than half-speed → **DC 12 DEX save** or fall through to Floor 3 (1d6 bludgeoning, prone)
- **Long rest blocked** in the entire house (dungeon)

### Flesh Mound options at the altar
- **Sacrifice a living creature** → mound eats it but stays bound (does NOT free the party)
- **Attack the mound** → screeches awake, full combat (depleted variant: HP 68 / AC 15)
- **Use the amber shard to command "Walter"** → DC 13 CHA Intimidation. Mound recognizes its name, can be released with mercy. (This is the campaign's preferred resolution.)

After resolution: house begins to collapse → party flees up to Floor 1 → out the front door (now unlocked or breakable) → into the Mists → wakes up on the road in **Barovia**.
