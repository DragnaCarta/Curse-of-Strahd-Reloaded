## Part A — Death House carryover (finish strong)

Full detail lives in `Campaign/Course/module_1/session_1/plot.md`. This section is the **cheat sheet**.

### Order of discovery (typical)

| Order | Location | What happens |
|-------|----------|----------------|
| 1 | **Floor 3 landing** | Read-aloud → **four cuirassiers** frenzy when someone steps on the balcony. Lethal shoves →3d6 bludgeoning + 2d10 piercing (dead at 0). |
| 2 | **Floor 3 library** | Klara’s resignation letter (pregnancy). **Amber shard** in niche opens secret door behind bookshelf. **Trap:** pulling the switch first → dart volley (DC 13 DEX, 2d6 piercing + 1d6 poison, poisoned on fail; dead at 0). *Consider disarming narrative if they use the shard first.* |
| 3 | **Secret room (behind library)** | Skeleton in chest. **Strahd’s letter** (full text below). First time they see the name *Strahd von Zarovich* — they **do not** recognize it. |
| 4 | **Floor 3 conservatory** | *Waltz for Klara* on harpsichord → vision → **Elisabeth’s Wrath**: DC 14 WIS or 3d8 psychic + frightened; **dead at 0** (heart stops). Also opens library secret if not open. |
| 5 | **Floor 4 attic** | Unstable floors (heavy armor or run → DC 12 DEX or fall to Floor 3). **Toy chest:** centipede swarm **before** skeletons. **Spare bedroom:** music box + **basement map**. **Storage:** real Klara → **specter** chases until basement stairs. |
| 6 | **Basement** | Cult chant, Flesh Mound, escape, collapse. **Level 3** when they **escape the house** (your Session 1 milestone). |

### Strahd’s letter (read aloud or hand out)

Use when they open the chest / read the letter in the **secret room** (library).

> *My most pathetic servant,*
>
> *I am not a messiah sent to you by the Dark Powers of this land. I have not come to lead you on a path to immortality. However many souls you have bled on your hidden altar, however many visitors you have tortured in your dungeon, know that you are not the one who brought me to this beautiful land. You are but a worm writhing in my earth.*
>
> *You say that you are cursed, your fortune spent. Your husband took solace in the bosom of another woman, sired a bastard son, and drove you to abandon love for madness. Cursed by darkness? Of that I have no doubt. Save you from your wretchedness? I think not. I much prefer you as you are.*
>
> *Your dread lord and master,*
>
> *Strahd von Zarovich*

**DM:** Players **don’t** know who Strahd is yet. Let the name land. The letter explains Elisabeth’s bitterness and ties the **amber cult** to a greater predator.

### Centipede “mob” (toy chest, Floor 4)

Not a separate statblock file — run as **Swarm of Insects** style (centipedes) from `plot.md`:

| Stat | Value |
|------|--------|
| **Attack** | +3 to hit, **4d4 piercing** to one target in the swarm’s space |
| **CON save** | DC 11 or **poisoned** 1 minute (disadvantage on attacks and checks) |
| **AC** | 12 |
| **HP** | 22 |
| **Resistances** | Bludgeoning, piercing, slashing from nonmagical weapons |
| **Weaknesses** | **Fire** and **area effects** (e.g. *burning hands*, *shatter*, alchemist’s fire, torch used creatively — reward clever play) |
| **Single-target attacks** | Deal **half damage** (swarm nature) |

**Tactics:** Swarm occupies **opener’s space** — claustrophobic, visceral. After it dies, reveal **Rose and Thorn’s skeletons**.

### Other quick-reference hazards (Death House)

- **Klara’s specter:** AC 12, HP 22; Life Drain +4, **3d6 necrotic**, max HP reduction until long rest; **dead if max HP → 0**. **Chases** through attic until destroyed or party hits **basement stairs** (cult barrier).
- **Basement path:** Shard opens library secret; **map** in attic spare room; **hidden door** in storage (DC 14 Investigation, or Rose if possessed).
- **Death at table:** Options in `session_1/plot.md` (spirit, Dark Gift, new PC, Rose/Thorn sacrifice).
- **Flesh Mound / sacrifice paths:** `session_1/plot.md` Scene 4 + `Campaign/NPCs/monsters/flesh_mound.md` (depleted variant for small parties).

## Part B — Arc B: Welcome to Barovia

When the **mists** spit them out onto the Svalich Road, run **`plot.md`** in this same folder (`module_1/session_2/plot.md`).

### Leveling (aligned with your Session 1 house escape)

- If you already award **Level 3** when they **escape Death House**, the party enters Arc B at **Level 3**.
- In **`session_2/plot.md`**, the siege scene awards **Level 3** only if they are still **below 3** (e.g. Death House unfinished). Otherwise give **inspiration** or a **short rest** moment instead — avoid double-bumping the same level in one session unless you intend to.
- Optional: award **Level 4** after burial + Doru resolution + commitment to Vallaki — only if your broader `level_scaling.md` schedule allows it.

### Opening recap — Barovia (read aloud or paraphrase)

Use when they emerge from the mists onto the Svalich Road (not the Death House recap).

> *Last time, you escaped from a nightmare — a house that breathed, a house that wanted you dead. You emerged into a land that feels wrong. The fog has not lifted. The sky, when you can see it, is overcast and colorless. You are no longer in Daggerford. You are somewhere else entirely. Ahead: the road continues west.*

### Key NPC reminders (don’t wing it cold)

- **Ismark:** Capable, grieving, exhausted — defers to the party from fatigue, not weakness. Line: *"They call me Ismark the Lesser. Not without reason."*
- **Ireena:** Not a quest objective — push back if ordered around. Bite scars, Strahd *recognized* her.
- **Doru:** Heartbreak > menace; lucid windows; whispers apologies. **Decide before session:** destroy / cure / leave unresolved — downstream plot changes.

### Timeline (in-game)

**Neyavr 1** evening → siege → **Neyavr 2** morning burial. Act II pressure: Vallaki by end of **Neyavr 2** if you follow default pacing (often Session 3–4 sessions).
