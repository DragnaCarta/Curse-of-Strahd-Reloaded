## What Rahadin Does
> Rahadin LETS the heist happen. He is testing the party. He files away every detail.
> He WILL report to Strahd: what they took, where they went, who they spoke to.
> If the party tries to fight him: he steps aside rather than fight (below 80% health). He wins every fight.

## The Beacon Lighting — Maximum Drama
- Use silence effectively — silence before the fire lights
- The beacon should feel like the campaign's midpoint climax
- Vladimir's death should be cathartic, not horrifying
- Strahd's storm response is the cliffhanger into Act IV

## Strahd Tyrant Persona Shift
> After the beacon lights, Strahd's character changes:
> - No more dinner invitations
> - No more polite curiosity
> - Nightly harassment: appears at camp, monologues about the party's failures, sends targeted threats
> - He does not strike directly — he makes the world around them harder
