## The Abbot — First Impression Notes
- He is NOT immediately threatening
- Play him as earnest, gentle, and slightly wrong
- The Vasilka glimpse should be unsettling purely through appearance — she is too still, too perfect
- His delusion about Strahd is one of the campaign's great tragic ironies

## Winery Combat Pacing
- 6 vine blights + 4 needle blights + 3 druids can be overwhelming
- Run it as two phases: exterior vineyard, then interior winery (druids cornered)
- The ritual countdown mechanic creates urgency — stress it

## Muriel / Keepers of the Feather Reveal
- This is a significant world-building moment — the players discover there IS a resistance
- Davian should feel like someone who has been doing this work quietly for decades, not heroically
- The wereraven reveal should normalize the Martikov family's nature naturally

## The Three Fanes — Plant the Seed
> The party should leave this session with a clear long-term objective:
> 1. Defeat Baba Lysaga → plant gem at Swamp Fane (Berez)
> 2. Gather allies → Forest Fane and Mountain Fane
> 3. This sets up Act IV
> Don't overwhelm with details — just give them the map

## Rahadin's Invitation Closing Hook
- Deliver it dramatically — a crisp envelope sealed with Ravenloft's crest
- Rahadin doesn't deliver it himself (he's too important for errands in most cases — but this time he did)
- Urwin: "He came in, left it on the bar, and no one in the room breathed for thirty seconds."
