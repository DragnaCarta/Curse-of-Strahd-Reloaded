## The Dinner — Tone Management
- This is not a trap. Strahd genuinely enjoys entertaining.
- There should be real food, real wine (that Van Richten warned them not to drink — the party must decide)
- The brides serve as subtext: their interactions with the party and each other reveal the Ravenloft politics
- Volenta should be contained chaos throughout — she's bored and the party is entertainment

## Varushka's Nightmare — Scripting
> The nightmare sequence is one of the campaign's most powerful exposition moments.
> Play soft, melancholy music underneath.
> Don't rush the visions — each one should be a vignette the party can react to.
> The Tatyana vision is key: if Ireena is with the party, she will recognize herself in Tatyana's face.

## Sasha's Timing
- She makes her move when Strahd is engaged in conversation with another PC
- She is desperate but controlled — she has been planning this moment for years
- If a PC is suspicious of her: she meets their eyes levelly. "I know how this looks. I know what I am. I am asking you to believe me anyway."

## Heist Preparation Hook
> End the session with the bridge collapsed and the party trapped.
> Give them Sasha's key and the information: Strahd is away tomorrow night.
> They wake at Level 7, fully rested, with one night to raid the castle.
