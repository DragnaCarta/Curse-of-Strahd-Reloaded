# Month 3 · Week 3 — Arc M: The Dragon's Manor + Arc O: Dinner with the Devil
**In-Game Date:** Neyavr 12–13  
**Party Level:** 11 → 12 (award Level 12 after surviving Strahd's dinner)  
**Location:** Argynvostholt → Castle Ravenloft (dinner)  
**Session Goal:** Visit Argynvostholt, meet Argynvost's ghost, receive the skull recovery request; attend Strahd's dinner and survive the nightmare overnight.

---

## Scene 1 — Argynvostholt
> "The manor is a ruin of silver and shadow. Once it was magnificent — a sprawling estate built by a silver dragon who took human form to walk among the people he protected. Now the roof sags, the great hall's rafters are exposed to grey sky, and the silver decoration has tarnished to near-black. But it is not empty. From within, you hear the clank of armor."

**The Revenants of the Order of the Silver Dragon:**
Armored undead knights patrol the manor — not hostile by default, but suspicious. They challenge the party: "Do you serve the devil of Ravenloft?" If the answer is no: they stand down, but watch.

**Sir Godfrey Gwilym (whom the party met on the road):** He is the Order's acting commander in the absence of **Vladimir Horngaard**.

**Vladimir Horngaard:** The previous commander, a revenant consumed by hatred. He sits in the great hall, staring at nothing, full plate armor rusted onto a frame that no longer needs to breathe. He is not an enemy today — but he is not an ally. He hates Strahd. He hates the party for thinking they can succeed where his Order failed.

> "You will fail," he says, without looking up. "Every hero who has come to this valley has failed. I watched them fail. I am still here. They are not." A long pause. "But if by some miracle you do not fail — bring our lord home. Bring Argynvost home. Let that much be undone."

**The Dragon Gargoyle:** In the manor's chapel, a stone gargoyle with a dragon's head. Touching it triggers a vision:

> "You see a battle three centuries ago — a silver dragon, enormous, fighting the vampire's forces on a clifftop. The dragon is losing. It knows it's losing. It fights anyway, buying time for the last of its Order to escape. The vampire's blade takes it through the throat. The dragon falls. And as it falls — as everything goes dark — it thinks one thing, the same thought three hundred years running: the beacon. Light the beacon. Let there be hope."

**Argynvost's Request:** The gargoyle channels the dragon's spirit. It asks the party to recover Argynvost's skull from Castle Ravenloft's Hall of Bones — it has been used as a trophy for 300 years. Return it to the mausoleum. Light the beacon.

**What the beacon does:** When lit, it burns with silver fire visible across all of Barovia. It grants a +1 bonus to AC and saving throws to anyone actively resisting Strahd. It also triggers Strahd's shift from **Gentleman** to **Tyrant** — he stops playing games.

---

## Scene 2 — The Dinner Invitation and Preparation
The party has the invitation from last session. Before attending:

**Sasha Ivliskova's Message:** A note slipped under the Blue Water Inn door (unsigned): *"Accept the invitation. Come. I will find you during dinner. I have information. — A friend inside."* This is Sasha, Strahd's eldest bride and secret traitor.

**Van Richten's Warning** (if party has found him): "Don't go. But if you must go — don't eat anything. Don't drink anything. Don't accept any gifts. Don't be alone with any of his brides. And whatever you do — do not challenge him in his own house."

---

## Scene 3 — Arrival at Castle Ravenloft (Dinner)
> "The castle rises from the clifftop as if it grew there — black stone, impossible towers, a bridge over a chasm that drops into fog. Gargoyles line the battlements. The bridge trembles slightly as you cross. But torchlight burns warm in the windows, and from within: music. An actual harpsichord. Someone is very good."

**Rahadin at the Door:**
> "A dusk elf in fine livery opens the great doors. He has the face of a man who has seen everything and been affected by nothing. A constant faint sound clings to him — you can barely hear it, but it's there: screaming. The screams of thousands of people, all at once, layered into a barely-audible whisper. 'I am Rahadin,' he says. 'Chamberlain. Welcome to Castle Ravenloft. The master is expecting you.'"

**The Court:**
- **Anastrasya Karelova:** Elegant in gold, dripping charm. Treats the party as honored guests while taking their measure.
- **Ludmilla Vilisevic:** Cold and analytical. Stands to the side. Asks questions that feel like tests.
- **Volenta Popofsky** (if she escaped from Vallaki): "Oh! YOU! This is delightful." She is manic with glee.
- **Escher:** Nervous, watchful, stays near Strahd. Catches one party member's eye meaningfully when Strahd isn't looking.
- **Sasha Ivliskova:** Older than the others. Quieter. She smiles at the party with eyes that say *wait for my signal*.

**Strahd at Dinner — The Gentleman at Peak:**
> "He is seated at the head of a table that could seat forty, set for eight. He stands when you enter, which no one of his status would do — it is a courtesy so deliberate it becomes a kind of dominance. 'Welcome,' he says. 'I've been curious about you. Come. Sit. Eat.'"

**Dinner Roleplay:** This is a pure roleplay scene. Strahd:
- Asks each player character a personal question and actually listens to the answer
- Mentions knowing things about their pasts they haven't shared
- Speaks about Barovia as if it were his beloved child, not his prison
- Briefly acknowledges Ireena if she's present — as if seeing her is both joy and pain

**Key Lines:**
> "I do not ask you to love this land. I ask only that you understand it. Barovia is not a curse. It is a consequence. My consequence. I would not change the choice I made — only the ending."

> *(to the party, at the end of the meal)* "I confess, I hope you succeed in whatever it is you came here to do. Life in Barovia needs its complications. You are very good at complications."

## Scene 4 — Sasha's Secret + Trapped Overnight
During dinner, Sasha finds a moment to pull one party member aside:
> "'He will be away tomorrow night — the new moon. He leaves every new moon to… attend to things I do not ask about. The castle is not unguarded, but he is not here. Whatever you need from this castle — do it tomorrow night.' She presses a small iron key into their hand. 'This opens the east crypt passage. Go before dawn. Tell no one I helped you.'"

**The Lightning Strike:** As dinner concludes and the party prepares to leave — a bolt of lightning strikes the bridge, collapsing it. The drawbridge mechanism is jammed. The party is stranded for the night.

> "Rahadin appears at the dining room door. 'An unfortunate accident,' he says, in the tone of a man describing a slightly overcooked meal. 'The master extends his apologies. Rooms have been prepared. You are guests. You will be treated as guests.'"

---

## Scene 5 — Varushka's Nightmare
In their rooms, the party sleeps — and is pulled into a shared dream by the ghost of **Varushka**, a former castle servant.

> "The dream begins in daylight — a version of the castle that is bright and warm. A young woman in a maid's uniform beckons you forward. 'Come,' she says. 'I will show you what this place was. Before.'"

The nightmare walks the party through Ravenloft's history:
- **Vision 1:** Strahd as a living general, victorious, celebrated
- **Vision 2:** His first sight of Tatyana — the moment the obsession began. He looks at her the way a man looks at the answer to a question he didn't know he was asking.
- **Vision 3:** The wedding. Sergei and Tatyana. Strahd watching.
- **Vision 4:** The murder of Sergei. The compact with the Dark Powers. The first kill.
- **Vision 5:** Tatyana throwing herself from the castle battlement rather than be with Strahd. His face as she falls. This is the campaign's thesis: not cruelty, but grief that curdled into everything cruelty is.

**Varushka's Final Words:**
> "I served him for twenty years before he killed me. I don't say that with anger anymore. I say it because you should understand — he does not think of himself as a monster. The worst ones never do." She fades. "Survive the night."

**Award:** End of session. Level 7 awarded at the start of Week 4 (the heist session begins at full strength).
