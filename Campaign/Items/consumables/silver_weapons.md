# Silver Weapons & Ammunition
**Category:** Consumable / Equipment (Bypass Resistances)  
**Sources:** Vallaki blacksmiths, Ezmerelda's wagon, Vistani traders  
**Price:** +100 gp to silver-plate a weapon; 1 gp per silver arrow/bolt

---

## Why Silver Matters
In Barovia, certain creatures are resistant or immune to non-magical, non-silver weapons:

| Creature | Resistance Bypassed by Silver | Notes |
|----------|------------------------------|-------|
| Werewolves | Yes — bludgeoning/piercing/slashing from non-magical, non-silver | Full resistance without silver |
| Wereravens | Yes | Same as werewolves |
| Vampire spawn | Non-magical resistance bypassed by silver | Magical weapons also work |
| Strahd | Immune to non-magical damage | Only magical weapons or silvered work |
| Rahadin | Standard resistance applies | Silver or magical needed |
| Flesh Mound (Death House) | Resistant to piercing/slashing | Bludgeoning recommended |
| Will-o'-wisps (Berez) | Immune to non-magical | Must use magical or silver |

---

## Silver-Plating Weapons
Any blacksmith can silver-plate a weapon for **100 gp** (1 week of work). In Vallaki:
- **The blacksmith on Festival Square** will do it; he doesn't ask questions
- His prices increase slightly after the festival falls (Market pressure; he's nervous)

**Silver-plated weapons:** The silver coating wears off eventually — after approximately **50 strikes** against a resistant target, make a DC 10 Athletics check. On failure, the silver is worn too thin to matter; treat as non-silvered.

---

## Ezmerelda's Stock
Ezmerelda carries a complete set of silvered weapons in her wagon:
- 4 silver daggers
- 2 silvered short swords
- 20 silver arrows (2 quivers)
- 10 silver crossbow bolts
- 1 silvered handaxe

She will share freely with allies. If asked to sell: 10 gp per silver arrow, 75 gp per silver melee weapon.

|---------|-------------------|------------|
| Month 1, Wk 4 | Strix wolves (Tser Pool) | Low — strix are fey, silver optional |

| Month 4, Wk 3–4 | Final battles | Very high — silver + magic |

---

## At High Party Levels (12+)
By Month 3, most party members will have +1 or better magical weapons, which bypass silver resistances entirely. Silver becomes less critical as an equipment concern — but silver arrows remain useful for archer characters who haven't found magic ammunition.

**DM note:** Don't belabor the silver economy at high levels. The "do you have silver?" tension is most effective in Months 1-2. By Month 3, the party should feel capable. Let them.
