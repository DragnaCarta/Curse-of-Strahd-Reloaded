# Wolfsbane (Aconitum)
**Category:** Consumable (Curative Herb / Combat Use)  
**Sources:** Ezmerelda's wagon; Lysaga's herb knowledge; Barovian herbalists (rare); forests outside Krezk  
**Price:** 25 gp per dose from Ezmerelda (she never charges full price for it — she knows what it's for)

---

## Description
Tall-stalked purple flowers with distinctive hooded blossoms. Beautiful. Genuinely beautiful. It grows at the edge of forests, especially near running water. The smell, up close, is acrid-sweet — pleasant at a distance, overwhelming close. The name comes from its original use as wolf repellent. The folkloric use came later.

In Barovia, most people know what wolfsbane looks like. Most children are taught before age six. It grows more abundantly here than anywhere else in the world, as if the land has been trying to provide a cure for as long as the curse has existed.

---

## Lycanthropy Cure
**Window:** Must be administered within **24 hours** of a lycanthrope bite that drew blood.  
**Method:** The bitten person ingests a tea brewed from wolfsbane flowers (or chews the flowers raw — less effective, same result if they can bear it).  
**Effect:** Purges the lycanthropy infection before it can take hold.  
**Constitution save:** DC 12. On failure: the wolfsbane doesn't take — the lycanthropy begins. Can try once more with a new dose (DC 14 on second try).

**After 24 hours:** Wolfsbane cannot cure an established infection. The only cure past that point is *Remove Curse* or the character embraces their lycanthropy.

---

## Embracing vs. Resisting Lycanthropy
**If the party's lycanthropy is not treated:**
- The character becomes afflicted at the next full moon
- They must make a DC 14 Wisdom saving throw or lose control during the transformation
- Afflicted lycanthropes cause their bite to be infectious to others

**If a character *chooses* to keep their lycanthropy:**
This is valid. Werewolves, werebears, and wereravens exist in Barovia as people with complex lives. The curse is what Kiril Stoyanovich did to the pack — not lycanthropy itself.

**Werewolf (curse):** Speed, Str bonus, pack tactics — but aggressive instincts, full moon transformation, and alignment pull toward Chaotic Evil  
**Werewolf (embraced):** Same stats, plus the character retains control during full moon with DC 15 Wisdom. They are no longer susceptible to non-silver weapons.

---

## Combat Use
Wolfsbane can be used in combat against lycanthropes:
- **Contact poison (applied to weapon):** DC 14 Con save; on failure, lycanthrope has disadvantage on attacks and is poisoned for 1 minute
- **Thrown (improvised):** DC 10 attack vs AC; direct contact with flowers forces DC 14 Con save or lycanthrope is frightened of the attacker until end of next turn
- **Area:** Scattering wolfsbane in a 5-foot area makes lycanthropes unwilling to move through it (DC 14 Wisdom or they go around)

## Notes for DM
- After any werewolf encounter where blood is drawn, privately note which character was bitten
- Give the player a chance to ask about treatment (if they don't know about wolfsbane)
- The 24-hour window creates appropriate urgency without being punishing
- If a player *wants* to become a lycanthrope for story reasons: let them. It's good drama.
