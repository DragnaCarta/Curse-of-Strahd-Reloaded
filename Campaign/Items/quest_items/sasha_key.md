# Sasha's Iron Key
**Category:** Quest Item (Access Key)  
**Source:** Sasha Ivliskova (slipped to a party member during the dinner, Month 3, Week 3)  
**Required For:** East crypt passage access during the Ravenloft heist  
**Session:** Given Month 3, Week 3; used Month 3, Week 4

---

## Description
A heavy iron key, old-fashioned, with a long shank and an ornate bow in the shape of a bat (ironic, given the source). It's cold — everything in Castle Ravenloft is slightly cold — but it's been worn smooth from years of handling.

---

## What It Opens
The east crypt passage: a secondary entrance to Castle Ravenloft's lower levels that bypasses the main entrance and the Great Hall entirely. It connects to a passage between the crypts and the dungeons.

**Without this key:** The party must enter through the main gates (past Rahadin) or find another route. The key saves approximately 20 minutes of navigation and one likely confrontation.

**With this key:** The party can reach:
- The crypt level (Icon of Ravenloft, Gertruda's cell) in 4 minutes from outside
- The dungeon level (Emil, Spear of the Huntress) via a connecting stair in 8 minutes
- The Hall of Bones requires going up one level — adds 5 minutes

---

## Sasha's Instructions
> "This opens the east crypt passage. The passage is not guarded after midnight — Rahadin checks it at eleven and assumes no one uses it. Come before dawn. Leave before the castle bells ring at six. Go."

She presses the key into a party member's hand during dinner, during a moment when Strahd is engaged with another player character. The transfer takes 2 seconds. She returns to her composed dinner-guest posture as if nothing happened.

---

## Notes
- The key is mundane iron — no magic, no special properties
- It can be copied by a skilled locksmith if needed (DC 15 Smith's Tools — but who in Barovia has a locksmith?)
- If a party member is searched before leaving Ravenloft after the dinner: DC 15 Sleight of Hand to keep it hidden, or Rahadin finds it. If found: Rahadin looks at it, looks at the party member, and says nothing. He files this away. He lets it happen.
- If lost: Sasha will not give another — she took a significant risk the first time
