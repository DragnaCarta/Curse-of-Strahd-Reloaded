# Bones of St. Andral
**Category:** Quest Item (Sacred Relics)  
**Status at Campaign Start:** Missing (stolen by Milivoj → sold to Henrik van der Voort → taken by Volenta)  
**Required For:** Restoring St. Andral's Church ward (Arc D, Month 2, Week 1)  
**Session:** Month 2, Week 1

---

## Description
A collection of finger bones and a fragment of jaw, contained in a small carved wooden box sealed with wax bearing the Morninglord's sun symbol. The box is simple, old, and incongruously ordinary for what it does.

When held by a person of faith (or near consecrated ground), the bones radiate faint warmth — the same quality as the Icon of Ravenloft, but quieter. More domestic.

---

## The Ward They Create
When the bones are embedded beneath St. Andral's altar:
- An invisible protective field covers the entire church grounds
- No undead can enter willingly or by force while the ward holds
- The ward feels like stepping inside on a cold day — the same quality, but spiritual
- Visible to Detect Magic: a pale golden dome

**Ward duration:** Indefinite, as long as the bones remain beneath the altar.  
**Ward failure:** If the bones are removed, the ward collapses immediately. Volenta's plan requires them removed before Neyavr 7 (St. Andral's Feast).

---

## The Chain of Custody
1. Bones embedded in the altar by Saint Andral himself (centuries ago)
2. Father Lucian knows they're there; shows new groundskeepers as part of orientation
3. **Milivoj** (groundskeeper, 17, siblings to feed) sells them to the coffin-maker for a handful of coins
4. **Henrik van der Voort** stores them in a locked chest (he thinks it's just old bones from a eccentric collector)
5. **Volenta Popofsky** takes them from Henrik when she moves into his shop
6. Party recovers them and returns them to Father Lucian

---

## Recovering Them
The bones are in a locked chest in the coffin-maker's shop, behind a replica altar Volenta built as a mockery.

- **Chest key:** On a chain around Henrik's neck. He gives it the moment Volenta is driven off.
- **Chest lock:** DC 12 Thieves' Tools as a backup.

**After recovery:** Father Lucian resets the bones beneath the altar with a brief ceremony (5 minutes). The ward hums back to life — visible to anyone with Detect Magic as a faint golden dome. Several Vallakians who happened to be outside the church stop and look up, not knowing why they suddenly feel slightly safer.

---

## Notes
These bones have no combat use and no magical properties outside of their ward function. Their importance is entirely narrative: they are the thing standing between Volenta's massacre and forty people's lives.

The party may not initially understand why old bones matter this much. Father Lucian's explanation is the best summary: "Saint Andral gave his bones so his people would have a safe place. He has kept that promise for three hundred years. I would like to keep it for another three hundred."
