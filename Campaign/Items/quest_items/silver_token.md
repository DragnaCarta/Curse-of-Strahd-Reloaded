# Zuleika's Silver Token
**Category:** Quest Item (Identification Token)  
**Source:** Zuleika Toranescu (given when the deal is struck, Month 3, Week 2)  
**Required For:** Getting the sympathetic dungeon guard to look away during Emil's rescue

---

## Description
A silver coin roughly the size of a thumb, but not a coin — it's been hammered flat and stamped with a wolf's paw print on one side and a crescent moon on the other. Simple craft. Obviously meaningful. The silver is slightly tarnished in the way of things that have been carried close to a person for years.

---

## The Deal
Zuleika gives this to the party when they agree to rescue Emil from Castle Ravenloft's dungeons. She tells them:

> "Show this to the guards at Ravenloft's dungeons. There is one guard who will recognize it. He is not Strahd's man."

---

## The Guard
The dungeon guard who recognizes the token is a human man named **Grigori** — middle-aged, Barovian, with a family in Krezk. He took the job at Ravenloft because it pays better than anything available in the valley, and his children need food. He loathes it. He has been quietly cooperating with Zuleika's network for two years.

When shown the token, Grigori looks at it, looks at the party, and looks away. He slides the cell key under the door.

> "I was never here. My family lives in Krezk. If you get out of here — tell them I did the right thing, at least once."

**His ask:** Tell his family (Marta and their two children — Petra and Dmitri, ages 8 and 11) that he loves them. That he's sorry. He doesn't believe he's getting out of this job alive.

**If the party follows through:** This is one of the small, quietly devastating moments. Marta Grigori is in Krezk. She knows. She's been waiting. When the party delivers the message: she closes her eyes for a moment. "He always said the job would kill him. I told him to quit. He said we needed the money." She opens her eyes. "Thank you."

---

## Notes
- The token has no magic. It is a piece of silver with a stamp on it. Its power is entirely relational.
- Grigori will not fight the party. He will not raise an alarm. He wants out of this as much as they do.
- If the party loses the token before the heist: Zuleika can make another one (it takes her 10 minutes with a hammer and a coin). The token's identity is the symbol, not the specific piece of silver.
