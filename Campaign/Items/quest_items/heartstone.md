# Night Hag Heartstone
**Category:** Quest Item (Hag's Focus)  
**Source:** Morgantha (Old Bonegrinder); must be borrowed or taken  
**Required For:** Victor's Ethereal Plane ritual (Arc H, Month 2, Week 4)  
**Duration of Need:** 1 hour (Victor returns it after the ritual or it's destroyed when Morgantha dies)

---

## Description
An egg-sized gemstone, purple-black and cold to the touch regardless of ambient temperature. It vibrates softly — not a feeling of magic, exactly, but of something that is in two places simultaneously. Looking at it too long in low light makes the viewer feel faintly watched.

Night hags use heartstones to slip between the Material Plane and the Ethereal Plane. They are deeply personal objects — part tool, part extension of the hag's spirit.

---

## How to Obtain It

### Option 1: Combat (Best for Resource-Spent Party)
Defeat Morgantha (or her coven). The heartstone is on her person — a cold lump in a belt pouch.  
**Risk:** If the full coven fights, this is a very hard encounter at Level 7-8. Best done during the day when one hag is out selling pastries (coven power breaks when split).

### Option 2: Theft (Skill Challenge)
Morgantha sleeps during midday hours. She has Truesight while dreaming — she sees through illusions and invisibility.  
**DC 18 Sleight of Hand** to take the heartstone without waking her. The Truesight means the character must physically appear to be doing something innocuous. Even then she may wake (Perception +8 passively).

### Option 3: Trade
Morgantha will consider a significant trade. She will NOT accept Ireena. She might accept:
- A life-debt from a party member (she chooses when to call it — dangerous)
- A unique magical item of value
- Information she doesn't already have (very hard — she knows a lot)
- Promise of non-interference with her operation (ethically questionable — the addicted children remain)

---

## Victor's Use
Victor needs the heartstone for exactly 1 hour to power the Ethereal Plane crossing ritual on the night of the full moon (Neyavr 9, in-game).

> "The heartstone glows as the midnight bell rings. The circle flares silver. The room goes translucent."

After the ritual: the heartstone can be returned to Morgantha (if trade or theft method), or kept (if combat method — Morgantha is dead).

---

## Properties (If Kept)
If the party keeps the heartstone after obtaining it:
- **Etherealness (Action, 1/day):** The holder can become Ethereal for up to 1 hour (as per the *Etherealness* spell). While Ethereal, can interact with the Ethereal Plane normally.
- **Dream Touch (Action, 3/day):** Touch a sleeping creature — that creature can be targeted by *Dream* or *Nightmare* (per the DM's choice of Spell)
- **Detect Intent (Bonus Action):** While holding the heartstone, the user can sense the surface-level emotional intent of one creature within 30 feet (not thoughts — emotions)

**Note:** If Morgantha is alive and the party has her heartstone, she will come to reclaim it — aggressively. She is patient; she will pick her moment.

---

## Connection to the Ethereal Plane
The heartstone is why Erasmus van Richten was able to be heard and seen by Victor during his spirit mirror experiments — the heartstone Victor was using (stolen from Morgantha previously, before she retrieved it) opened a crack between planes. Victor doesn't have it anymore, which is why Erasmus had to reach out to the party instead.
