# Holy Symbol of Ravenkind
**Category:** Artifact (Legendary Wondrous Item)  
**Acquired:** Month 3, Week 2 — from Zuleika Toranescu after rescuing Emil from Castle Ravenloft  
**Foretold by:** Madam Eva's Tarokka reading (Card 2)

---

## Description
A golden sunburst amulet on a silver chain. It radiates faint warmth even at rest — real warmth, physical, noticeable. Up close, it smells faintly of pine forests and open sky. The craftsmanship is ancient; no jeweler in Barovia could have made it.

Zuleika Toranescu has kept it wrapped in cloth because holding it bare causes physical discomfort to lycanthropes (it is not painful — it is simply overwhelming, like staring at the sun).

---

## Properties
**Requires attunement by a creature of good alignment.**

- **Radiant Light (Action, 1/day):** The amulet blazes with real sunlight in a 30-foot radius for 1 minute. This counts as direct sunlight for vampires and creatures with Sunlight Sensitivity.
- **Invoke (Action, 3/day):** All undead within 30 feet must succeed on a **DC 17 Wisdom saving throw** or be:
  - Frightened of the wielder for 1 minute (Wisdom save at end of each turn to end)
  - Take **3d8 radiant damage** on a failed save (half on success)
- **Passive Protection:** While wearing the symbol, the wielder has **advantage on saving throws against vampire Charm and Frightened effects**
- **+2 bonus to all saving throws** while held (bonus increases to +3 in the presence of undead of CR 10 or higher)
- **Detect Undead:** The amulet pulses once per minute when undead are within 60 feet

| Use | Effect |
|-----|--------|
| Radiant Light (Action) | 1-minute sunlight aura — triggers Strahd's sunlight sensitivity, suppresses his Regeneration |
| Invoke | DC 17 Wis frightened + 3d8 radiant (or half) to Strahd and all spawn/brides in range |

**Best use:** Attune a party member who doesn't need another action-economy item. The passive save bonus is always valuable. Save the active uses for the final fight.

## How to Get It
Zuleika Toranescu holds the symbol. She will give it when Emil is safely returned from Castle Ravenloft's dungeons. The exchange happens at the werewolf den after the Ravenloft heist (Month 3, Week 4).

**The deal (Month 3, Week 2):** Party promises to rescue Emil → Zuleika promises the symbol upon Emil's return.

**If the party rescues Emil but doesn't return to the den:** Zuleika sends a raven requesting the meeting. She will not chase them. She trusts they will come.

---

## Lore
The symbol was crafted by the Ladies Three — specifically by the Seeker — as a gift to the clerics of the Morninglord who served Barovia's people before Strahd's arrival. The clerics hid it rather than let it fall into Strahd's hands. Over centuries it moved from hiding place to hiding place.

The wereravens (Keepers of the Feather) eventually gave it to the werewolf pack for safekeeping — werewolves being one of the few things in Barovia with both the strength to protect it and the nature to keep it hidden from vampires (the symbol is painful to vampiric undead to even look at directly).
