# Father Lucian Petrovich
**Type:** Human Priest (Stat Block: Priest, CR 2)  
**Role:** Vallaki's Moral Anchor; St. Andral's Church  
**First Appears:** Month 2, Week 1 (St. Andral's Church, Vallaki)  
**Inspiration:** Uncle Iroh (Avatar: The Last Airbender), Mister Rogers, a man whose faith is genuinely, quietly earned

---

## Appearance
Approximately fifty. Grey at his temples, laugh lines around his eyes that haven't been used as much lately. He has the face of a man who gives you his full attention when you speak to him — like you're the only person in the world.

## Personality
Genuinely warm. Not performatively warm — warm in the way that comes from decades of choosing to believe in people despite evidence to the contrary. He has held Vallaki together through the Baron's insanity, Strahd's shadow, and generations of accumulated despair. He is tired. He is still here.

He is not naive. He knows what Barovia is. He has buried a lot of people. His faith in the Morninglord is not certainty — it is a choice he makes each morning. This makes it more admirable, not less.

**Voice:** Unhurried, warm, occasionally gently funny.

**Key Lines:**
- "I'm glad you're here. I need help. I need it quietly."
- *(to Milivoj, after his confession)* "You were afraid and hungry. That is not a sin I can condemn."
- *(when the bones are restored)* "Thank you." *(presses the relics to his chest)*
- *(after the bones are restored, quietly)* "I don't know how much longer I can hold this place together. But while these walls stand — so do I."
- *(at dawn, after the victory)* *(no words — just his face turned up to the sun)*

## St. Andral's Church
The church is the only place in Vallaki that most citizens feel genuinely safe. The protective ward (from St. Andral's bones beneath the altar) creates a real aura of safety — not just psychological.

**The Ward:**
- Requires the bones of St. Andral embedded beneath the altar
- While active: no undead can enter the church grounds
- If broken (bones removed): collapses completely; church is vulnerable
- If restored before Neyavr 7: holds through the Festival night

---

## Arc D: The Stolen Bones
Father Lucian discovered the bones missing three mornings before the Festival. He has told no one — afraid of the Baron's reaction. His quiet request to the party to recover them quietly is the arc's inciting event.

His forgiveness of Milivoj (the teenager who sold the bones out of desperation to feed his siblings) is one of the campaign's early demonstrations that genuine good exists in Barovia.

---

## Campaign Arc
- **Act II:** Central character of Arc D. Recovers from the bones crisis; continues as community anchor.
- **Act III:** Present in Vallaki through all political changes. Works with Lady Wachter's new regime.
- **Act IV:** Leads civilian protection at the war council. Present when the dawn comes.
- **Epilogue:** His final moment — on his knees in the town square, arms wide, face turned to the first real sunrise in 400 years.
