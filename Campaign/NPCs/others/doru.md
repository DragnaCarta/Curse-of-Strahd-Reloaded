# Doru
**Type:** Vampire Spawn (potentially cured)  
**Role:** Father Donavich's Son; Vampire Spawn Fighting His Nature; Potential Redemption Arc  
**First Appears:** Month 1, Week 2 (Church basement, Village of Barovia)  
**Inspiration:** Zuko (Avatar: The Last Airbender), Jesse Pinkman (Breaking Bad)

---

## Appearance
Nineteen years old, dark hair, pale as wax. Veins visible under skin. Eyes that flash red when hungry. Wears the remnants of the clothing he left home in — still recognizably his, now wrong.

## Personality (Lucid Moments)
Terrified of himself. Self-aware enough to know what he's become, not in control enough to manage it. His lucid windows are brief and they are devastating — when they come, he is just a scared teenager who misses his father and his betrothed and his life.

He led the charge to Castle Ravenloft after being inspired by Van Richten. He was the first through the gate. Strahd made an example of him: turned him into a vampire spawn and sent him back home.

**Voice (lucid):** Barely a whisper. He apologizes a lot.

**Key Lines (lucid):**
- "Father… I can hear you praying. I don't deserve it."
- "Tell Gertruda I'm sorry. Tell her not to wait. Tell her — " *(loses lucidity)*
- *(if cured)* "Is this real? Is this—" *(first deep breath in weeks)*
- *(if destroyed)* *(peaceful face returning)* "...thank you."

---

## Stat Block (Vampire Spawn)
**AC:** 13 (natural)  
**HP:** 82 (11d8 + 33)  
**Speed:** 30 ft., climb 30 ft.

**Damage Resistances:** Necrotic; bludgeoning, piercing, slashing from nonmagical weapons  
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft.

**Regeneration:** Regains 10 HP per turn if below max HP (suppressed in sunlight or by radiant damage)  
**Spider Climb:** Can climb walls/ceilings  
**Vampire Weaknesses:** Sunlight hurts, running water hurts, repelled by garlic

**Multiattack:** Two Claws + Bite  
**Claws:** +4, 2d4+2 slashing  
**Bite (grappled/restrained/willing):** +4, 1d6+2 piercing + 2d6 necrotic

**Note:** Doru only attacks if a player gets within 5 feet of the cell bars OR if he smells blood. His attacks are not predatory — they are reactive, driven by hunger he cannot control.

---

## Three Paths (Chosen in Month 1, Week 2)

### Path 1: Destruction
Father Donavich, with the party's help, unlocks the cell. Doru has a final lucid moment. He thanks someone by name for telling Gertruda he's sorry. He looks peaceful when it's done.

**Consequence:** Father Donavich's arc becomes grief → acceptance → rebuilt faith. He will be present at the dawn, older and quieter.

### Path 2: Cure (Remove Curse)
Requires *Remove Curse* + DC 16 Religion check to channel the Morninglord's grace. Works — barely.

**Consequence:** Doru is freed from vampire spawn status. He is weakened, haunted, and will need months to recover. He can become an NPC ally in later acts — present at the war council with Father Donavich, helping defend the church.

**Note:** Escher (Strahd's consort) was Doru's friend before. If Doru is alive in Act III or IV: an encounter between them is possible. Escher's complicated feelings surface.

### Path 3: Unresolved
Doru continues to suffer. Father Donavich continues to pray. This becomes a ticking clock — within 7 in-game days, Doru escapes and feeds. This creates an undead incident in the village.

---

## Gertruda
Doru's betrothed. She comes to Vallaki to find him in Act II and eventually reaches Castle Ravenloft (where Strahd keeps her as a guest/prisoner). She is one of the heist objectives in Month 3, Week 4.
