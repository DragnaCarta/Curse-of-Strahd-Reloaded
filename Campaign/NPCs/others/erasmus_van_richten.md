# Erasmus van Richten
**Type:** Ghost (Child); Stat Block: Ghost (CR 4) — but never hostile  
**Role:** Van Richten's Dead Son; Arc H Catalyst; Emotional Core of the Van Richten Subplot  
**Appears:** Month 2, Week 3 (Blue Water Inn — first contact); Month 4 (final moment)

---

## Appearance
A boy of about fourteen, translucent, wearing the clothing he died in. He carries a ball that he drops and reaches for and never catches — it passes through his hands and falls through the floor. He has not noticed this in quite some time. He is cheerful in the way of ghosts who haven't fully accepted their state yet.

## What Happened
Erasmus van Richten was Rudolph van Richten's son. The Radanavich Vistani clan, in retaliation for Van Richten's actions against them, took Erasmus and sold him to a vampire lord. Van Richten tracked the vampire down and destroyed it — but not in time. Erasmus was already turned. Van Richten destroyed his son to save him.

Erasmus's ghost drifted to Castle Ravenloft because he was looking for something — he isn't entirely sure what. He has been there, wandering, for years.

## Personality
Cheerful in a way that is both sweet and heartbreaking. He misses his father. He knows his father misses him. He has made his peace with being dead in a way that his father has not made peace with.

He is perceptive — he sees things others miss, including Stella's scattered soul in the Ethereal Plane, which is why he sought out the party.

**Voice:** Bright, curious, occasionally trailing off as he loses track of a thought (ghost trait). Then snapping back.

**Key Lines:**
- "You can see me! Most people can't see me. My name is Erasmus."
- "There's a girl. A living girl whose soul is lost in the Ethereal Plane. She's been calling for help for so long."
- *(on his father)* "He doesn't know I'm here. He thinks about me though. I can tell."
- *(if the party asks if he wants to move on)* "Not yet. I want to see my dad one more time. When it's over."
- *(final moment, the note he leaves Van Richten)* "I forgive you, Dad." *(Van Richten finds this in his wagon after the final battle)*

---

## Campaign Role
- **Month 2, Week 3:** He appears through the inn wall and leads the party to Victor. He explains Stella's situation.
- **Month 2, Week 4:** Present during the Ethereal Plane ritual. He helps guide Stella's soul back when Leo Dilisnya is defeated — he knew where she was the whole time.
- **After Stella's rescue:** He hangs around less. He seems more settled.
- **Final Battle / Epilogue:** He leaves a note in Van Richten's wagon. Van Richten finds it. This is the most quietly devastating moment in the epilogue.

---

## The Note (Final Moment)
After the victory, Van Richten finds a folded piece of paper in his wagon that wasn't there before. In a child's handwriting: *"I forgive you, Dad. I always did. — E"*

Van Richten does not speak for a long time.

---

## Stat Block (Ghost, CR 4) — Nonhostile
**AC:** 11 | **HP:** 45 | **Speed:** 0 ft., Fly 40 ft. (hover)  
**Incorporeal Movement:** Can move through creatures/objects (takes force damage if ending turn inside)  
**Ethereal Sight:** Sees into Ethereal Plane  
**Possession:** Standard ghost possession (DC 13 Cha to resist) — **Erasmus will never use this against the party**

He can use Possession only to communicate with someone who cannot see him otherwise, and only with explicit in-game consent.
