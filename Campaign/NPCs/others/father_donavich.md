# Father Donavich
**Type:** Human Priest (Stat Block: Priest, CR 2 — though he will not fight)  
**Role:** Village of Barovia's Priest; Doru's Father; Faith in Crisis  
**Appears:** Month 1, Week 2 (Church, Village of Barovia)  
**Inspiration:** Eddard Stark carrying a burden that breaks him, Boromir trying to hold a line

---

## Appearance
Grey-haired, gaunt, eyes permanently red from weeping and sleeplessness. He answers the door clutching a holy symbol he no longer fully believes in — the gesture is habit, the faith behind it is fraying. He has not slept in days.

## Personality
A good man who has been broken by an impossible situation. He loves his son. He believes his prayers can cure Doru. He is wrong about the cure but right about the love. He cannot stop because stopping means admitting his son is lost, and he is not ready to do that.

He is not angry at the party. He is barely present — grief has taken up most of the space inside him.

**Voice:** Hollow, slow. He speaks like someone operating on memory of how to speak rather than present engagement.

**Key Lines:**
- *(opening the door)* "I have nothing left to give. Whatever you want — I have nothing."
- *(on Doru)* "He was brave. That's what they never say. He was the bravest one who went. He went first."
- *(if the party asks to cure Doru)* "Can you? Can you actually — " *(doesn't finish the question; afraid of the answer)*
- *(if Doru is destroyed)* "Was it… was it peaceful?" *(when told yes)* He closes his eyes. He doesn't speak for a long time.
- *(at the dawn, end of campaign)* "He would have liked this." *(to no one)*

---

## Arc Through the Campaign
**Month 1, Week 2:** He is broken. He refuses to let Doru be destroyed (at first). With the party's compassion, he may allow it — or the party may cure Doru by other means.

**If Doru is destroyed:** Donavich grieves through Act II. He begins rebuilding his faith in Act III — slowly, quietly. By Act IV he is present at the war council in spirit (he defends the church), and when the dawn comes, he is on his knees in the churchyard.

**If Doru is cured:** Donavich and Doru have a painful, tender reunion. Donavich spends weeks helping Doru recover. By Act III, Doru is stable enough to assist with church defense. Donavich looks ten years younger.

**If unresolved:** Doru eventually escapes the church (7 in-game days) and feeds. Donavich is devastated. The church's spiritual anchor in Barovia village cracks.

---

## Stat Block
He has the standard **Priest** stat block but will not use it aggressively. He may use *Healing Word* or *Guiding Bolt* in desperate defense of the church or party, but only if directly attacked.

**AC:** 13 (chain shirt) | **HP:** 27 | **Spellcasting DC:** 13  
Key spells: *Sanctuary, Healing Word, Sacred Flame, Spare the Dying*
