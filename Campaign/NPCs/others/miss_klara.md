# Miss Klara — The Nursemaid
**Location:** Death House, Floor 2 (Nursemaid's Suite) — appears in the mirror  
**Role:** Benign spirit; information source; tragic figure  
**Session:** Month 1, Week 1  
**Classification:** Ghost (specter origin) — NOT hostile

---

## Appearance
A young woman in humble servant's clothes, dark hair simply pinned back. Pretty in a quiet way. Her spirit appears only as a reflection in the mirror in the Nursemaid's Suite — she cannot leave the glass, cannot speak aloud. She communicates by nodding and shaking her head.

Her corpse is found in a trunk in the attic — she starved to death. The wounds on the spirit's body match. Elisabeth locked her in.

---

## Personality
Patient. Gentle. Deeply sad. She has been in that mirror for a very long time with no one to talk to. When the party appears she is visibly relieved — not frightened of them, immediately attentive, eager to help in the only way she can.

She is not afraid of the party. She is afraid of Mrs. Durst's name.

---

## What She Knows
She can answer yes/no questions by nodding or shaking her head. She knows everything she knew in life:

| Question | Her Answer |
|---------|-----------|
| "Are you Miss Klara?" | Nods |
| "Did you work here?" | Nods |
| "Are you dead?" | Long pause. Nods. |
| "Did Mrs. Durst do this to you?" | Visible fear. Nods. |
| "Was Walter your child?" | Closes her eyes. Nods slowly. |
| "Is there something dangerous in the basement?" | Urgent nodding. |
| "Is Mrs. Durst down there?" | Shakes head. Hesitates. Points down. Then up. Complicated. |
| "Is there a way out?" | Nods. Points downward. Then makes a running gesture. |

---

## Her Story
Miss Klara was the Durst family nursemaid — and Gustav Durst's mistress. Walter, the infant whose corpse is at the heart of the Flesh Mound, was their child. Elisabeth Durst discovered the affair, used her amber shard to have the cult deal with Gustav, and had Miss Klara locked in the attic trunk where she starved.

She has been in the mirror since. She knows Elisabeth's amber shard controls the Flesh Mound. She knows where the basement ritual chamber is. She knows what happened to every member of this family.

She cannot say any of it out loud. But she will answer every question the party thinks to ask.

---

## DM Notes
- Her reveal is most effective if the party finds the corpse in the attic BEFORE seeing the mirror — the moment they realize the corpse and the reflection are the same person
- She is one of the most emotionally resonant moments in Death House if played quietly
- She does not follow the party out of the mirror — she stays, even after the house collapses
- If a player asks "will you be okay?" she looks at them for a long moment. Then she smiles, just slightly. Then she steps back from the glass until she disappears.
- She never answers the question
