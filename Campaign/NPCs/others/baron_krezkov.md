# Baron Dmitri Krezkov
**Type:** Human / Werewolf (Stat Block: Werewolf, CR 3)  
**Role:** Burgomaster of Krezk; Secret Werewolf; Gate-Keeper of Act II's Final Area  
**Appears:** Month 2, Week 4 (Krezk village gates)

---

## Appearance
A large man, late forties, with a soldier's build gone slightly soft from years of administration. His hair is greying. His handshake is too firm. He has the look of someone carrying a secret that has gotten heavier with every passing year.

## Personality
Formal, guarded, protective of his village to the point of paranoia. Krezk's walls are his life's work — he has kept people safe inside them. He does not open those walls easily.

He is not cruel. He is simply not willing to trust strangers without a reason, and his definition of "reason" is specific: be useful.

**The Secret:** He is a werewolf. Not by choice — he was bitten years ago. He has managed his curse through willpower and the blessing of Mother Night, whom he quietly venerates. His wife knows. Most of Krezk's inner circle suspects. No one says anything because he is a good burgomaster and the alternative is chaos.

His son Ilya has inherited the lycanthropy and ran away to the werewolf den near Lake Baratok, where Kiril's pack found him. Baba Lysaga's scrying confirms this. Dmitri does not know how to feel about this.

**Voice:** Controlled, formal, with occasional slips when the topic is Ilya.

**Key Lines:**
- "These gates are closed to travelers. Krezk protects its own. If you want to enter, bring me something useful."
- *(when given Baba Lysaga's answer about Ilya)* "He went willingly." *(a long pause)* "He went willingly."
- *(if the party asks about his condition)* "I manage."

---

## The Entry Requirement (Arc I)
Dmitri will open Krezk's gates ONLY after the party completes his request: travel to Berez and have Baba Lysaga scry for his missing son Ilya. He provides a personal item of Ilya's — a carved wooden horse Ilya made as a child.

This requires a brief, non-hostile interaction with Baba Lysaga (Month 2, Week 4 — her first meeting with the party).

---

## His Wife: Anna Krezkova
She manages the village's day-to-day administration while Dmitri handles defense and diplomacy. She is warm, practical, and has been covering for her husband's condition for years with quiet efficiency. She and the party will likely never have a significant scene, but she is the person who actually makes Krezk function.

---

## Ilya Krezkov
His teenage son. He ran to the werewolf pack because he was frightened of his lycanthropy and thought they could help him understand it. Kiril's pack is not the right place for a frightened teenager seeking guidance, but it was the only werewolf community Ilya knew about.

**If the party rescues Emil and Zuleika takes over the pack (Arc L, Arc Q):** Ilya can return home. The pack under Emil is a healthier environment — he may remain connected to the pack community while also returning to Krezk.

**Dmitri's reaction to Ilya's return:** Restrained. He grips his son's shoulder. He does not speak. He does not need to.

---

## Stat Block (Werewolf, CR 3 — if somehow forced into combat)
He will never fight the party. If Krezk is under attack, he transforms to defend the walls.

**AC:** 12 (humanoid), 11 (wolf form) | **HP:** 58  
**Multiattack (Hybrid):** One Bite + Two Claws  
**Bite:** +5, 2d6+3 piercing + lycanthropy curse (DC 12 Con)
