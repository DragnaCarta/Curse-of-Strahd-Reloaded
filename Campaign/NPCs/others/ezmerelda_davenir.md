# Ezmerelda d'Avenir
**Type:** Vistana Monster Hunter (Custom Stat Block, CR 8)  
**Role:** Foretold Ally (Tarokka); Party Combat Specialist  
**First Appears:** Month 3, Week 2 (breaking into Abbey of Saint Markovia)  
**Inspiration:** Michonne (*The Walking Dead*), Inigo Montoya (*Princess Bride*)

---

## Appearance
Mid-thirties. Vistana heritage. Quick, precise movements. A prosthetic leg from the knee down — she maintains it herself and has installed a hollow silver compartment in it for weapons. She enters through windows when there's a perfectly good door, because she prefers knowing the layout before committing to an entrance.

## Personality
Sharp, warm, dry-humored. She has been doing this work alone for years since Van Richten pushed her away, and she has made peace with that — mostly. She is not bitter about the leg; she is matter-of-fact about it. She is the most competent person in most rooms she enters, and she is politely aware of this without being obnoxious about it.

**Voice:** Fast, direct, occasionally sarcastic, genuinely warm underneath.

**Key Lines:**
- "I was going to ask who you are, but you're clearly not Strahd's people and you're clearly not dead yet, which puts you ahead of most visitors to this abbey. I'm Ezmerelda d'Avenir. I'm a monster hunter."
- "It's a prosthetic leg. It's fine. It's actually better than the original in several ways." *(demonstrates by producing a silver stake from the hollow)*
- *(to Van Richten, when revealed)* "You pushed me away to save my life. I know. You were right and you were wrong simultaneously. Now let's not have this conversation while there are monsters." *(she hugs him awkwardly, then lets go)*

## Stat Block (CR 8)
**AC:** 17 (studded leather + shield)  
**HP:** 120 (16d8 + 48)  
**Speed:** 30 ft. (prosthetic leg — no speed penalty, she's used to it)

**STR** 14 (+2) | **DEX** 18 (+4) | **CON** 16 (+3) | **INT** 16 (+3) | **WIS** 14 (+2) | **CHA** 14 (+2)

**Saving Throws:** Dex +7, Int +6, Wis +5  
**Skills:** Acrobatics +7, Insight +5, Investigation +6, Perception +8, Stealth +7, Survival +5

### Special Abilities
- **Favored Enemy (Undead):** +2 to damage vs. undead; can reroll 1s on damage dice against undead.
- **Silver Arm:** Her prosthetic can deliver silver-treated unarmed strikes (+4, 1d6+4 bludgeoning, counts as silvered).
- **Monster Lore:** Ezmerelda can make an Intelligence (History) check (DC 10) to recall abilities, weaknesses, and lore of any monster she has encountered before.

### Actions
- **Multiattack:** Two attacks with her rapier or hand crossbow.
- **Rapier:** Melee +7, 1d8+4 piercing + 2d6 (sneak attack vs. undead, always applies)
- **Hand Crossbow (silvered bolts):** Ranged +7, 1d6+4 piercing, range 30/120
- **Silver Stake Drive (Action):** Melee, +7 to hit, 2d6+4 piercing, automatically grants Paralyzed condition to a vampire (per vampire stake rules)

### Equipment
- Wagon with supplies north of Krezk: wolfsbane (10 doses), holy water (12 vials), wooden stakes (30), silvered weapons (rapier + 20 crossbow bolts), her research journals
- Prosthetic leg with silver hollow

## The Foretold Ally
If Ezmerelda was the Tarokka's ally card, she recognizes the party:
> "The Hooded One card. Madam Eva told me. I've been waiting for this group for two years."

She knows about the Tarokka reading because Madam Eva also reads for her people. She has been working independently toward the same goal.

- **Arc K (Month 3, Week 2):** Introduction at the Abbey. Joins the party.
