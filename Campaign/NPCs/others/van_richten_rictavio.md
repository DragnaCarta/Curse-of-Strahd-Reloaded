# Dr. Rudolph van Richten (Alias: Rictavio)
**Type:** Human Wizard/Fighter hybrid (Stat Block: Modified Archmage, CR 12)  
**Role:** Legendary Vampire Hunter; Ezmerelda's Former Mentor; Van Richten's Guide  
**First Appears:** Month 2, Week 2 (in disguise as Rictavio, Vallaki)  
**Inspiration:** Van Helsing, Sherlock Holmes in retirement, a tired superhero

---

## Appearance (Rictavio Disguise)
Wide-brimmed hat, theatrical makeup, fake nose, elaborate false mustache. A carnival-master persona layered over someone who is clearly not a carnival master. His eyes, even behind the disguise, miss nothing.

## Appearance (True Form)
Older — late sixties. Grey hair, hard weathered face. The kind of man who has been wounded so many times the scars have stopped telling a story and just become skin.

## Personality
He pushed away everyone he loved to protect them from the Radanavich curse (a curse laid on him: everyone he loves dies by a monster's claws). His son Erasmus died. His former apprentice Ezmerelda lost her leg. He has been alone for decades and tells himself this is fine. It is not fine.

He is brilliant, capable, and emotionally armored to a degree that is both his greatest strength and his damage.

**Voice:** Dry wit underneath constant professional wariness. He deflects personal questions with information.

**Key Lines:**
- *(as Rictavio)* "I wouldn't do that if I were you. The tiger gets cranky when strangers knock."
- *(when identity suspected)* "Rictavio is simply a humble carnival master who—" *(slight pause if his name is said)* "—who has no idea what you're talking about."
- *(when revealed, to Ezmerelda)* "I pushed you away to save your life. I was right and I was wrong simultaneously."
- *(in his note to Ezmerelda at the end)* "I'm sorry I was too afraid. I'm proud of you."

---

## The Radanavich Curse
Arturi Radanavich cursed Van Richten after Van Richten's actions (unintentionally) led to the deaths of the Radanavich clan. The curse: anyone Van Richten loves deeply dies by a monster's claws.
- His son **Erasmus** was taken by the Radanaviches and turned undead. Van Richten destroyed him.
- Erasmus's ghost haunts Castle Ravenloft (Arc H).
- The curse is real. This is why Van Richten is alone.

---

## Arabelle Situation
Van Richten recognized Arabelle's divine Seeker bloodline (from his research). He correctly identified Strahd's interest in her. He hid her in his wagon. His method was wrong — hiding her without telling her family. His assessment was right.

## Stat Block (Modified, CR 12)
**AC:** 12 (15 with Mage Armor)  
**HP:** 99 (18d8 + 18)  
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**Spellcasting** (Int +9, DC 17): Prepared spells from the Wizard list.  
**Signature Spells:** *Sunbeam, Vampiric Touch (understands it to fight vampires with their own power), Counterspell, Mislead, Banishment*

**Special Equipment:**
- A stake of vampire-slaying (acts as a +2 dagger against undead)
- Wolfsbane tinctures (enough for 6 doses — cures werewolf curse before full moon)
- Silver-tipped bolts (hand crossbow)
- Van Richten's Guide notes (the party can find relevant monster information by asking him)

---

## Campaign Arc
- **Act II:** In disguise; helps from a distance; rescues Arabelle on his own initiative.
- **Act III:** May reveal himself if pressed; admits who he is to the party.
- **Arc K (Month 3, Week 2):** Full reveal. He and Ezmerelda have their reunion.
- **Act IV:** Active party support. He fights in the final assault.
- **Epilogue:** He finds a note from Erasmus's ghost — "I forgive you, Dad." He doesn't speak for a long time.
