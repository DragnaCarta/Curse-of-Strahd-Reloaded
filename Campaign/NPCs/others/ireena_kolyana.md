# Ireena Kolyana
**Type:** Human Fighter (Stat Block: Veteran, CR 3)  
**Role:** Primary Protected NPC; Tatyana Reincarnation; Ismark's Adoptive Sister  
**First Appears:** Month 1, Week 2 (Burgomaster's Mansion, Village of Barovia)  
**Inspiration:** Belle, Éowyn, Hermione Granger, Katniss Everdeen

---

## Appearance
Early twenties. Auburn hair, green eyes. Two bite marks on her neck (scarring from Strahd's attack). Travels in practical clothing — she is not a delicate figure. She carries a sword and knows how to use it.

## Personality
She is stubborn, brave, warm, and quick to argue with anyone who tries to treat her like a package to be delivered. She cares more about others than herself — she accepted leaving Barovia not for her own safety but because Ismark convinced her it was for the people she'd leave behind.

She is not passive. In every dangerous situation, she wants to fight. The party must convince her to stay back, not hold her back by force.

**The Reincarnation:** She does not know she is Tatyana reborn. She does not know why Strahd looks at her with that particular expression. If she ever finds out: process it slowly, over multiple sessions. It is a lot to absorb.

**Voice:** Direct, warm, slightly impatient with nonsense.

**Key Lines:**
- "I'm not a package to be delivered."
- "He looked at me like he recognized me. Like I was something he'd lost. It was the most terrifying thing I've ever felt."
- "Where exactly is safe in Barovia? Tell me. I'll pack a bag."
- *(on Izek, after the sibling reveal)* "He might have been different. If things had been different." *(only this, then silence)*

---

## Campaign Arc
- **Act I:** Meets the party. Agrees to travel to Vallaki. Buries her father.
- **Act II:** Present in Vallaki. Izek's obsession targets her. The sibling reveal, if it happens, reshapes her understanding of her own history.
- **Act III:** Present at Strahd's dinner if the party brings her (risky — powerful scene). Watching Varushka's nightmare may show her Tatyana's face.
- **Act IV:** She is the Dark Powers' offered bargain. She refuses instantly and angrily. She is one of the most decisive characters in the campaign.
- **Epilogue:** She stands at the edge of the lifted fog and looks east. "What's out there?" "Everything," Ismark says.

---

## The Dark Powers' Bargain (Arc U)
> The Dark Powers offer to take Ireena's soul in exchange for Strahd's imprisonment and the fog lifting.
> 
> Ireena refuses. "I am not a price. I am a person."
> 
> If a PC considers saying yes: have Ireena look at them. "Don't you dare."
