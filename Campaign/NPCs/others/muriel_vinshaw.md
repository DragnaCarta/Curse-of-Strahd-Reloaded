# Muriel Vinshaw
**Type:** Wereraven (Stat Block: Scout + Raven hybrid, CR 1/2 scout / CR 3 in hybrid)  
**Role:** Keepers of the Feather Spy; Early Party Companion; Davian Martikov's Estranged Network Member  
**First Appears:** Month 1, Week 3 (Tser Pool road — silver-winged raven following the party)  
**Full Reveal:** Month 1, Week 4 (transforms during werewolf ambush)

---

## Appearance (Human Form)
Young woman, dark eyes, quick hands. She dresses practically and moves like someone who has been navigating uncertain situations her whole life. She is cheerful in a trickster-adjacent way — ravens are not gentle birds, and she shares their sensibility.

## Appearance (Raven Form)
A raven with silver-tipped wings. She is one of the ravens that has been following the party since the Svalich Road.

## Personality
She deflects personal questions. She finds the party genuinely amusing. She is loyal to the Keepers of the Feather and to the valley's people but has been estranged from the Martikov family proper — the reason is never fully explained, though Davian's "I thought you were dead" implies she vanished without notice at some point.

She is casual about her wereraven nature: "I'm a wereraven. We're different from werewolves. We chose our gift."

**Voice:** Quick, light, occasionally cryptic in the way of someone who keeps secrets for a living.

**Key Lines:**
- "I'm Muriel. I'm… a friend of friends. The ravens watch this valley. You've done things worth watching."
- "I'm a wereraven. We chose our gift." *(matter-of-fact)*
- *(when Davian sees her)* "Davian." "Muriel. I thought you were dead." "You should have come home." *(silence)*

---

## Keepers of the Feather
A network of wereravens who serve the Seeker goddess by protecting Barovia's people — carrying messages, providing safe passage, gathering intelligence. The Martikov family (Urwin, Davian) are the network's core. Muriel is a field operative.

Their sigil: a raven with silver-tipped wings.

## Campaign Arc
- **Act I-II:** Follows the party as a raven; transforms during the werewolf ambush; introduces herself.
- **Act II onward:** Travels with or near the party; provides aerial reconnaissance.
- **Arc J (Month 3, Week 1):** Her history with Davian is revealed. She fights in the Yester Hill assault, drawing druid attention at a critical moment.
- **Final Battle:** Present as aerial support.
