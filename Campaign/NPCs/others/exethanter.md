# Exethanter
**Type:** Ghost (Former Lich Archmage); Stat Block: Archmage (CR 12) ghost variant  
**Role:** Amber Temple's Former Master; Tome of Strahd Decoder; Guide to the Sunsword  
**Appears:** Month 4, Week 2 (Amber Temple)

---

## Appearance
An ancient figure in archaic robes — the style is centuries out of date. Translucent, drifting slightly off the floor. He fades in and out of visibility as his concentration lapses. Without memories, his presence in the world is faint.

When his memory is restored: he becomes more solid. Not physically, but in presence — he fills the room differently.

## Personality (Without Memory)
Confused, gentle, reaching for context that isn't there. He knows his name. He knows he was important here. He knows something terrible happened. He cannot access the narrative that connects these facts.

**Personality (With Memory Restored):**
Sad, sharp, purposeful. Centuries of grief compressed into a few hours. He mourns his students, his order, his failure. He also becomes immediately useful — he remembers everything.

**Voice (without memory):** Uncertain, trailing off.  
**Voice (with memory):** Precise, tired, exact.

**Key Lines (without memory):**
- "You are new. Have I met you? I cannot — I cannot quite —"
- "My name was Exethanter. I was the master here. Before the betrayal. Before…"
- "What year is it?"

**Key Lines (with memory restored):**
- "The betrayal. Vilnius. My apprentice. He took my phylactery to end me. He succeeded."
- "I taught the cipher to Strahd. When he was young and clever and came here to learn. Before the bargain. I should have — I should have seen."
- "The Vampyr sarcophagus. I know how to approach it safely. I should have destroyed it centuries ago."

---

## Restoring His Memory
The party must find **Vilnius's journal** in the temple's library (DC 15 Investigation to locate among centuries of accumulated papers) and read it aloud to Exethanter.

The journal records the betrayal — Vilnius's decision to destroy his master, his reasoning, the method. Reading it aloud to Exethanter lets him finally access the memory.

**The Moment:**
> "He reads in silence — or rather he listens, because he cannot hold the book. His eyes change as the memories surface. Something in his posture settles. When it's done, he is quiet for a long time. 'I see,' he finally says. 'I have been here for — ' he stops. 'I don't know how long. Long.' He looks at his hands. 'My students. They're all gone, aren't they.'"

---

## What He Offers
**The Tome of Strahd (Decoding):**
> "I know this cipher. I taught it to him — to Strahd, when he was young and came here to study. He invented it for private correspondence. I can read it." With an hour's work, he decodes the Tome entirely, revealing the Grand Conjunction plan.

**The Sunsword Retrieval:**
He can draw a Circle of Protection (from Evil) around the Vampyr's sarcophagus, preventing the Vampyr from possessing anyone who enters the circle. This makes the fight with Patrina possible without the Vampyr interfering.

**Temple Navigation:**
He knows every vestige, every trap, every hidden room. He is an extremely valuable guide. He cannot leave the temple — his ghost is bound here.

---

## The Amber Temple's History (From Exethanter)
Long ago, a group of good-aligned wizards captured the vestiges of dead evil gods and imprisoned them in amber sarcophagi. The plan: seal them forever. The problem: proximity to that much concentrated evil corrodes even the most resistant minds. The wizards began to go mad. They destroyed themselves and each other.

Only Exethanter survived — as a lich, with his memories intact, maintaining the seals. Then Vilnius betrayed him. The seals have been degrading since.

**The corruption miasma in the temple is real:** DC 14 Wisdom save hourly. Exethanter is immune (he IS the temple, in a sense). He can warn the party and advise them to take breaks.

---

## Stat Block (Ghost Archmage, CR 12)
He will not fight. His combat capability is theoretical — in practice, he is a guide.

**AC:** 12 | **HP:** 99 (from his Archmage stats; as a ghost, he cannot be permanently killed — he simply disperses and reforms)  
**Spellcasting (Int +9, DC 17):** Full Archmage spell list  
**Ghost traits:** Incorporeal Movement, Ethereal Sight, Possession (will not use)
