# The Martikovs — Keepers of the Feather Leadership
**Type:** Wereravens (Stat Block: Commoner/Guard + Wereraven hybrid)  
**Role:** Winery + Inn owners; Secret Resistance Network  
**First Appears:** Month 1, Week 4 (Urwin, at the Blue Water Inn)

---

## Urwin Martikov
**Location:** Blue Water Inn, Vallaki  
**Role:** Innkeeper, Keepers of the Feather mid-level operative

### Appearance
Heavy-built, dark-haired, wearing an innkeeper's apron that always seems slightly too small. He has the look of someone who is paying more attention than he lets on.

### Personality
Warm to guests. Dry-humored. He reads the party immediately — "You look like trouble. We like trouble here. Carefully, quietly." He is protective of his family, loyal to the network, and quietly proud.

**Voice:** Conversational, relaxed. The watchfulness is underneath, not on top.

**Key Lines:**
- "You look like trouble. We like trouble here. Carefully, quietly."
- *(when the party returns from Tser Pool)* "The ravens tell me you came from Madam Eva's people. That means something to us."
- *(to Davian, about a long-standing argument)* *(loud argument about something inconsequential)*

### Stat Block
Use **Guard** + Wereraven hybrid (fly 50 ft., silver-tipped beak, immunity to nonsilver nonmagical physical damage in raven/hybrid form).

---

## Davian Martikov
**Location:** Wizard of Wines Winery  
**Role:** Winery patriarch, Keepers of the Feather senior operative

### Appearance
Older than Urwin. Grey-haired, weathered, with the bearing of someone who has been in charge for a long time and is comfortable with it. He does not smile easily. He thanks people by arguing with them.

### Personality
Gruff, proud, occasionally difficult. He is the kind of person who shows care through criticism and shows respect through trusting someone with a hard job. His reaction to the winery being liberated is to immediately argue with Urwin about something. This is how he expresses relief.

**His weight:** Davian's great-great-grandmother planted the first Weaver's gem. Five generations of his family have fought to protect this place. When the gem is finally replanted at the Swamp Fane: "It took us five generations. Three hundred years." He walks away before his composure breaks.

**Voice:** Blunt, low, occasionally softened by the weight of what he's carrying.

**Key Lines:**
- *(to Muriel)* "Muriel Vinshaw. I thought you were dead." "You should have come home." *(pause)*
- *(at the Swamp Fane)* "My great-great-grandmother planted the original. She died defending this place." ... "Thank you." *(walks away)*

## The Martikov Network
The Keepers of the Feather operate through:
- **Ravens:** Every raven in Barovia is either a Keeper or a Keeper's familiar/contact. Notes addressed to "DM" (Davian Martikov) reach him within 1 hour.
- **The Winery:** Financial and logistical base. The wine trade funds the network.
- **The Inn:** Intelligence hub. Nearly every traveler passes through the Blue Water Inn.
- **Muriel:** Field operative, currently embedded with the party.

**What they offer the party:**
- Safe message delivery
- Intelligence on Strahd's movements (ravens see everything)
- Safe harbor at the inn

---

## The Three Life-Gems
The Wizard of Wines grows magical grapes through three enchanted gems embedded in the vineyard soil. These same gems are needed to reconsecrate the Three Fanes (Act IV). The Martikovs have been their guardians for five generations.

| Gem | Status | Needed At |
|-----|--------|-----------|
| Gem 1 | Stolen to Yester Hill → recovered by party (Arc J) | Forest Fane, Yester Hill |
| Gem 2 | Secured at winery | Swamp Fane, Berez |
| Gem 3 | Secured at winery | Mountain Fane, Mt. Ghakis |
