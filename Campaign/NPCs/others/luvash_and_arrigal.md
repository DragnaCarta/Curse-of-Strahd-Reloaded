# Luvash & Arrigal
**Type:** Vistani (Stat Block: Bandit Captain CR 2 / Assassin CR 8)  
**Role:** Vistani Camp Leaders; Arabelle's Father and Uncle  
**Appears:** Month 2, Week 2 (Vistani camp east of Vallaki)

---

## Luvash
**Role:** Arabelle's father; camp leader; desperately loving parent

### Appearance
A broad man, Vistani heritage, with a broken arm from a recent altercation (he broke it trying to search for Arabelle himself and fell). His eyes are red. He has not slept.

### Personality
Loud, fierce, loving, and right now completely beside himself. His public face is authority — he is the camp's leader. His private face, with Arabelle, is a father who would burn down the world for his daughter.

He is not sophisticated. He feels things entirely and shows them entirely. This is both his greatest strength and the reason Arrigal has been able to operate in his shadow for years.

**Voice:** Loud, quick, emotional. He talks with his hands. He switches between demanding authority and desperate pleading mid-sentence.

**Key Lines:**
- "My daughter! Arabelle! She's gone! Gone from the camp three days!"
- *(to the guards)* "You say she's JUST a Vistani girl — I am Luvash, and I am calling in every debt this city owes my people!"
- *(when Arabelle is returned)* *(he doesn't speak — he just holds her, his whole body shaking)*
- *(after composing himself)* "Name your price. Anything. I mean anything."

### Stat Block (Bandit Captain, CR 2)
**AC:** 15 (studded leather) | **HP:** 65  
**Multiattack:** Three attacks  
**Scimitar:** +5, 1d6+3 slashing

---

## Arrigal
**Role:** Luvash's brother; camp co-leader; Strahd's spy

### Appearance
Lean, watchful, with a calm that reads as steadiness until you notice it never changes regardless of context. He is always slightly too composed for the situation.

### Personality
He is genuinely fond of his brother. He genuinely loves Arabelle. This makes what he does worse: he has been feeding information to Strahd about the camp for years, including Arabelle's location when she went missing. He told himself he was protecting the camp — cooperation with Strahd means the camp is left alone. He is not entirely wrong. He is also not entirely right.

The one line he would not cross: actively harming Arabelle. When Van Richten had her, Arrigal knew. He didn't retrieve her himself. He didn't give her exact location to Strahd because he knew what Strahd wanted her for.

**Voice:** Measured, quiet, direct. Watchful eyes that absorb everything without returning much.

**Key Lines:**
- *(to the party, aside while Luvash is distracted)* "My brother will offer you the stars. Bring her back alive and unhurt. Whatever Van Richten has told you about Vistani — remember that a father's love is the same in any language."
- *(if exposed as a spy)* "I did what I thought kept my people safe. I was right about the method and wrong about the cost. I know the difference now."
- *(if asked directly whether he works for Strahd)* *(long pause)* "I have survived in this valley for thirty years. How do you think I did that?"

### Stat Block (Assassin, CR 8)
**AC:** 15 (studded leather) | **HP:** 78  
**Special:** Sneak Attack +4d6, Assassinate (advantage + auto-crit on surprise)  
**Multiattack:** Two Shortsword attacks  
**Shortsword:** +6, 1d6+4 piercing

**Note:** Arrigal will not fight the party unless they expose him AND he believes they intend to harm the camp. Even then, he prefers to vanish.

---

## Exposing Arrigal
If the party discovers Arrigal's betrayal (through Arabelle's prophetic hints, investigation, or Strahd revealing it):

**Luvash's reaction:** Devastation. He is the kind of man who feels betrayal physically. He does not harm Arrigal — he is too shocked, and Arrigal is his brother. He exiles him instead.

**Arrigal's reaction:** He does not lie about it when confronted with proof. He explains. He does not beg. He accepts exile.

**Arabelle's reaction:** She knew. She didn't know what to do with knowing. She tells her father: "I always thought you'd handle it better than me."

---

## The Reward (for returning Arabelle)
Luvash offers:
- A trunk of gold (500 gp worth of Vistani jewelry and coin)
- A cart horse
- "A favor worth more than all of that" — the Vistani can travel freely through the Mists. If the party ever needs extraction or intelligence from outside Barovia, Luvash can arrange it.
