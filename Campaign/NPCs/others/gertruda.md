# Gertruda
**Type:** Human Commoner (Stat Block: Commoner, CR 0)  
**Role:** Doru's Betrothed; Ravenloft Prisoner; Heist Objective  
**Appears:** Month 3, Week 4 (Castle Ravenloft cell, during the heist)

---

## Appearance
Early twenties. She looks like someone who came here with a plan and has been adapting the plan on the fly for three weeks. Pale but intact. There's a book open in her lap when the party finds her.

## Personality
Direct. Practical. Not traumatized in the way the party might expect — she came to Ravenloft *willingly*, she has been mostly ignored by Strahd, and she has been reading his library while waiting. She is actually slightly annoyed to be rescued if the party implies she's been helpless.

She came to find Doru. She found what was left of him (or the aftermath of him, depending on what the party did in Month 1). Strahd found her. He found her tiresome and put her in a comfortable cell with books.

**Voice:** Brisk, collected, slightly impatient with drama.

**Key Lines:**
- "You're not Strahd's people. You're alive. How many of you are there?"
- "I came here voluntarily. I had reasons. I still have them, they've just changed shape somewhat."
- "He hasn't hurt me. He's mostly ignored me. I think he finds me uninteresting, which is the best compliment I've ever received."
- "I would like to go home now."
- *(if asked about Doru)* "I know what happened to him. I need to know which version it is."

---

## What She Knows
Three weeks in Strahd's library is not nothing. Gertruda has been reading. She doesn't understand most of it (much is in languages she doesn't speak or cipher) but she has been paying attention. She can describe:
- The layout of the castle's public levels
- Which guards are human vs. undead
- That Strahd was away more than usual in recent weeks
- That there is a room deep below she was told never to enter and described via gesture as "where the red light comes from"

This last piece of intelligence is a gift: she has identified the Heart of Sorrow's location (approximately).

---

## If Doru Was Destroyed
She knows before the party can explain. Something in their faces.

She doesn't cry in front of them. She says: "Thank you for telling me. Was it — was he — " They will have the answer to this from Month 1. She needs to hear it.

She carries grief with quiet dignity. She does not blame anyone. She goes home to the Village of Barovia and takes over her household. She visits Doru's grave regularly.

---

## If Doru Was Cured
Reunion, eventually. It is not simple — he is not the person she remembers and he knows it. It takes time. But the capacity for it is there.

She is the one who tells him: "You are still you. The part that matters. I have been here this whole time. I'm not going anywhere."

---

## Campaign Arc
- **Month 3, Week 4:** Rescued during the Ravenloft heist.
- Returns to the Village of Barovia.
- Contributes her map observations to the party's intelligence on Ravenloft's layout.
- **Epilogue:** Present in Barovia village when the dawn comes.
