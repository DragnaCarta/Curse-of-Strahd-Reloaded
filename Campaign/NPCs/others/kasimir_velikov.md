# Kasimir Velikov
**Type:** Dusk Elf Elder / Arcane Wizard (Stat Block: Archmage at lower CR, CR 7)  
**Role:** Guide to the Amber Temple; Grief-Driven Antihero; Long-Term Party Companion  
**First Appears:** Month 2, Week 2 (near Vistani camp, Vallaki)  
**Arc:** Act II introduction → Act IV climax

---

## Appearance
Grey-robed, lean, and clearly carrying a grief too large to set down. His eyes are old. A platinum signet ring glows faintly — connected to Khazan's Tower and the Amber Temple. He sits apart from everyone, always.

## Personality
Single-minded to the point of damage. Everything he does serves one goal: reaching the Amber Temple to find his sister Patrina. He is not unkind — he simply has no bandwidth for anything that isn't that goal.

He is not a villain. He is a man who loved his sister, watched her be destroyed (by her own people, then by Strahd), and has spent three centuries constructing a purpose around recovering her. He cannot stop because stopping means accepting the loss.

**Voice:** Quiet, precise, occasionally sharp when anything delays his purpose.

**Key Lines:**
- "You are going to Ravenloft eventually. I know this because you are the kind of people who end up in Ravenloft. When you do — there is a crypt. St. Markovia's crypt. There is something there I need."
- "My sister made a terrible mistake. Her own people killed her for it. Then Strahd killed what remained. I have been planning this for three hundred years."
- *(at the Amber Temple, when Patrina withdraws)* "She's still in there." *(not angry, just broken)*

---

## Patrina Velikovna (Sister)
Patrina was a dusk elf archmage of extraordinary talent. She offered to help Strahd achieve immortality — not from love, but from a belief that knowledge and power were worth any price. Her own people stoned her to death. Strahd turned her corpse into a wight and then destroyed the wight — "an act of mercy," he said.

Her spirit hid inside the Vampyr's sarcophagus in the Amber Temple, using it as a phylactery of sorts. The Sunsword hilt was with her.

**Kasimir does not know:** That Patrina is linked to the Sunsword. That to free the Sunsword, the party must fight her. That she cannot be simply restored — she must be fought first.

**When he finds out:** This is Kasimir's lowest moment. He does not attack the party. He steps aside. He says nothing for a long time. Then: "Do what you must. She would have understood. I don't, but she would have."

## The Dusk Elf Backstory
Once, dusk elves lived freely in the valley. When Strahd arrived, their matriarch refused to swear loyalty. Strahd killed her. **Rahadin**, then a dusk elf himself, assisted. Rahadin was exiled by his own people. The surviving dusk elves fled — the remnants now live under Vistani protection near Vallaki.

Kasimir is their de facto elder. He feels responsibility for them. His pursuit of Patrina is, in part, also a form of running from that responsibility.
