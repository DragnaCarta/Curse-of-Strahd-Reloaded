# Zuleika Toranescu & Emil Toranescu
**Type:** Werewolves (Good-aligned); Priestess and Former Pack Alpha  
**Role:** Providers of the Holy Symbol of Ravenkind; Pack Politics  
**First Appears:** Month 3, Week 2 (Werewolf Den, Lake Baratok)

---

## Zuleika Toranescu
**Role:** Priestess of Mother Night; Acting Pack Leader (under Kiril's de facto rule)

### Appearance
A tall woman with iron-grey streaks in dark hair. She has the calm of someone who has been waiting for a very long time without losing the thread.

### Personality
Devout, pragmatic, quietly powerful. She serves Mother Night — the patron of lycanthropes — as a force of balance, not terror. She believes the pack exists to protect, not to prey. Kiril has corrupted this. She has not been strong enough to stop him alone.

**Voice:** Deliberate, serious. She speaks as someone who does not waste words.

**Key Lines:**
- "I knew you would come. Mother Night showed me."
- "My mate Emil is in Ravenloft's dungeons. Strahd put him there to punish our pack for resisting."
- "If you bring him home — the Holy Symbol of Ravenkind is yours. Our people will pledge to stay neutral in whatever war is coming."

### What She Offers
- The **Holy Symbol of Ravenkind** — a golden sunburst amulet that radiates real radiant light — in exchange for Emil's rescue from Ravenloft's dungeons.
- The pack's **neutrality** in the coming conflict (they will not fight for Strahd).

### The Silver Token
She gives the party a silver token to show a guard in Ravenloft's dungeon — someone with family in Krezk who will help for 5 minutes if the party promises to get his family out.

---

## Emil Toranescu
**Role:** Former Pack Alpha; Prisoner in Castle Ravenloft

### Appearance
Large, with wolf-gold eyes and the look of someone who has been in a cage but hasn't let the cage change them. He is thin from deprivation. He is not broken.

### Personality
Steady, warm, quietly authoritative. He was the pack alpha because he deserved to be — not through violence but through leadership. He lost the position because Kiril ambushed him with Strahd's backing.

**In the dungeon cell:**
> "Zuleika sent you. I knew she would."

He will fight alongside the party if freed before the dungeon escape. His gratitude is genuine and expressed practically — "Tell me what you need and I'll do it."

---

## The Holy Symbol of Ravenkind
**Properties:**
- Radiates real bright sunlight in a 30-foot radius (vampires take sunlight damage while in this light)
- As an action, the wielder can invoke it: all undead within 30 ft. must succeed on a DC 16 Wisdom save or be frightened and take 3d8 radiant damage (half on save)
- +2 bonus to all saving throws vs. undead abilities while held

**Key:** This is one of the three foretold artifacts. The Tarokka reading should have pointed to the werewolf den as its location.

---

## Pack After Kiril's Defeat (Arc Q)
When Kiril is defeated and Emil returns:
- Emil retakes the alpha role without drama
- Several younger pack members choose to leave and integrate with human communities
- The pack pledges full neutrality to the party's cause
- Zuleika gives the party the Holy Symbol after Emil is safely returned
