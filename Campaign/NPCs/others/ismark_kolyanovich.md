# Ismark Kolyanovich
**Type:** Human Fighter (Stat Block: Veteran, CR 3)  
**Role:** Ireena's Adoptive Brother; Early Act I Companion  
**First Appears:** Month 1, Week 2 (Burgomaster's Mansion, Village of Barovia)  
**Inspiration:** Jon Snow, Faramir

---

## Appearance
Late twenties, broad-shouldered, dark hair, haunted eyes. Carries himself like a man who is perpetually braced for bad news.

## Personality
Quiet, direct, self-deprecating. He carries the weight of his ancestor's legacy — "Ismark the Great" — without believing he deserves to share the name. He is capable and loyal and genuinely good, but he can't see those qualities in himself because the context he lives in makes them feel insufficient.

**Voice:** Measured, honest. He doesn't perform confidence. He says what he thinks.

**Key Lines:**
- "They call me Ismark the Lesser. Not without reason."
- "Ireena didn't choose to look like whoever she looks like. That's not her fault."
- "My father died trying to keep the undead out with a sword. He was fifty-eight years old. He deserved better than this land."
- *(to Ireena at the epilogue)* "Everything."

---

## Stat Block (Veteran)
Identical to Ireena's — both are trained fighters, both have been defending Barovia together.

**AC:** 17 (chain mail + shield)  
**HP:** 58 (9d8 + 18)  
**Multiattack:** Two Longsword attacks  
**Longsword:** +5, 1d8+3 slashing

---

## Campaign Role
- **Act I:** Travels with the party from the Village of Barovia to Vallaki, escorting Ireena.
- **Act II onward:** He can stay in Vallaki or return to Barovia village (DM's choice). He is useful as a supporting combatant if kept around, but his primary narrative purpose is Act I.
- **Epilogue:** He is with Ireena when the fog lifts.
