# Victor Vallakovich
**Type:** Human Arcane Student (Stat Block: Apprentice Wizard, CR 3 modified)  
**Role:** Baron's Son; Forbidden Magic Student; Stella's Rescue Key; Morally Grey Ally  
**First Appears:** Month 2, Week 3 (his hidden workshop, via Erasmus's ghost)  
**Inspiration:** Draco Malfoy, Artemis Fowl

---

## Appearance
Seventeen. Bookish, messy hair, ink-stained fingers, the kind of face that defaults to narrow-eyed suspicion. He is clearly his father's son in appearance and not at all in personality (the Baron is bluster; Victor is precision).

## Personality
Arrogant and insecure in equal, simultaneous measure. He is genuinely talented in forbidden magic — he taught himself from his father's library and has gone further than most trained wizards would go. He knows this and it protects him from humility.

He will not apologize for his methods. He might, under enough pressure, admit he should have asked for help. The crack in his armor is Stella — he genuinely cares about her, and this care is the most honest thing about him.

**Voice:** Clipped, precise, slightly condescending until something matters to him, at which point the precision drops and you see the seventeen-year-old underneath.

**Key Lines:**
- "You can see Erasmus? Right. Fine. Then you know about the mirror."
- "I didn't ask for your opinion of my methods."
- "I know what I did wrong. I know the exact line I crossed. I crossed it because crossing it seemed like the only way to help her and I'd do it again."
- *(looking at Stella from across the room after her rescue)* *(says nothing)*

---

## The Spirit Mirror Incident (Arc H Background)
Victor found forbidden mirror-magic in his father's library — a technique for communicating with the Ethereal Plane. He adapted it, intending to use it to contact the dead (presumably his mother, who died when he was young). Instead, the mirror cracked during a session with Stella — her soul was pulled into the Ethereal Plane and scattered.

He has spent weeks trying to fix it. He hasn't been able to. He needs: a heartstone, a full moon, and help (which he was too proud to ask for).

## Stat Block (Modified, CR 3)
**AC:** 12 (15 with Mage Armor)  
**HP:** 28 (7d8 - 7)  
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**Spellcasting (Int +6, DC 14):**  
*Detect Magic, Mage Armor, Magic Missile, Mirror Image, Misty Step, Phantasmal Force, See Invisibility*

**Ethereal Plane Focus (special):** Victor can spend a spell slot of 1st level or higher to create a window into the Ethereal Plane (visible radius 30 ft.) for 1 hour. On the full moon, this becomes a crossing point.

---

## Campaign Arc
- **Act II (Month 2, Week 3):** Introduction via Erasmus. Assists with the spirit mirror ritual.
- **After Stella's restoration:** He withdraws to his workshop. He is processing. He visits Stella.
- **Act IV:** He is at the war council. He has something to prove. He proves it — his defensive magic is significant during Vallaki's siege.
- **Epilogue:** He tells Stella how he feels. Badly. She laughs.
