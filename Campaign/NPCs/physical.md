## Sourcing Notes

### Where to Get Minis
| Source | Best For | Cost Range |
|--------|----------|-----------|
| **Hero Forge** (heroforge.com) | Custom NPC minis (Ireena, Rahadin, party members) | $15–$30/mini |
| **WizKids D&D minis** | Pre-painted monsters (werewolves, vampire spawn, zombies) | $5–$15/mini |
| **Reaper Miniatures** | Unpainted; good NPC variety; often cheaper | $3–$12/mini |
| **MyMiniFactory / Printables** | 3D print files; huge Ravenloft selection | Free–$10/file |
| **Dwarven Forge** | Premium modular terrain; use for Castle Ravenloft | $$$; investment piece |
| **Fat Dragon Games** | Foam terrain kits; affordable | $40–$100/set |
| **2D printable maps** | Roll20, DriveThruRPG, Dungeon Fog | $5–$15; easiest option |

### Priority Purchase Order (Budget-Conscious)
1. **2D maps** for Death House and Castle Ravenloft — cheap, covers the most critical set pieces
2. **Vampire spawn** WizKids pack — used 3 acts, worth the investment
3. **Werewolf** pack — Month 3 critical combat
4. **Strahd** mini (WizKids has an official one) — the face of the campaign
5. **Ireena** custom via Hero Forge — she's at every session
6. Night hags, wolves, zombies — bulk WizKids sets
