# Strahd's Brides — Castle Ravenloft Court
**Location:** Castle Ravenloft  
**First Appears:** Month 3, Week 3 (Dinner at Ravenloft)

---

## Overview
Strahd has five brides (vampire spawn elevated to near-full-vampire status) who form his inner court. Each has a distinct role, personality, and degree of loyalty. They are not interchangeable.

---

## Ludmilla Vilisevic
**Type:** Vampire (modified, CR 8)  
**Role:** First Bride; Cold Analyst; Master of Undead and Ritual Magic

### Appearance
Tall, pale, white-blond hair always perfectly arranged. She dresses in grey and silver. She looks like the kind of person who has never made an impulsive decision.

### Personality
Analytical to the point of emotional nullity. She processes everything — the party, Strahd's mood, the political situation — as data. She is not unkind; she simply has no patience for inefficiency.

**Voice:** Measured, precise. She finishes every sentence.

**Key Lines:**
- "You have survived longer than the statistical average. Interesting."
- "The master finds you entertaining. I find you a variable."

### Stat Block Summary
**AC:** 16 | **HP:** 110 | **CR:** 8  
**Spells:** *Animate Dead, Counterspell, Dispel Magic, Hold Monster* (DC 16)  
**Actions:** Multiattack (2 Claws + Bite), Animate Undead (3/day creates 2d4 skeletons/zombies)

### Allegiance
Loyal to Strahd through genuine respect. She has calculated that Strahd is her best long-term outcome.

---

## Anastrasya Karelova
**Type:** Vampire (modified, CR 7)  
**Role:** Third Bride; Political Operator; Charming Face of the Court

### Appearance
Warm, olive-skinned, dressed in gold and red. She looks like she could run a merchant empire. She probably could.

### Personality
Genuinely charming — not a performance. She is interested in people. She is also measuring them for advantage at all times. She is Strahd's public-facing diplomat.

**Voice:** Warm, social, quick to laugh. The laugh is real. So is the calculation behind the eyes.

**Key Lines:**
- "Welcome! Please, sit. I understand you've been having quite an adventure."
- *(during the heist, passing the party in a corridor)* "You have done this before. Or you will. Time is strange here." *(steps aside)*

### Stat Block Summary
**AC:** 16 | **HP:** 95 | **CR:** 7  
**Spells:** *Charm Person, Dominate Person, Mirror Image* (DC 15)  
**Actions:** Multiattack (2 Claws + Bite), Charm (DC 15 Wis)

### Allegiance
Loyal to Strahd through political calculation. She would survive his fall — she is very good at surviving.

---

## Sasha Ivliskova
**Type:** Vampire (modified, CR 7)  
**Role:** Eldest Bride; Secret Traitor; Party Ally

### Appearance
The oldest-seeming of the brides — centuries of undeath have given her an air of settled sadness rather than predatory grace. She dresses in muted colors.

### Personality
She has been Strahd's bride the longest. She was not willing. She has been waiting for someone with a chance of succeeding.

**Voice:** Quiet. Measured. She speaks like someone choosing every word carefully because words have gotten people killed before.

**Key Lines:**
- *(slipping a note under the door)* "Accept the invitation. Come. I have information." 
- *(pulling a player aside)* "He will be away tomorrow night — the new moon. I have not told anyone. I am telling you."
- "I know how this looks. I know what I am. I am asking you to believe me anyway."

### Stat Block Summary
**AC:** 15 | **HP:** 82 | **CR:** 6 (she will NOT fight the party)

### Allegiance
She is the party's inside contact. She has been a traitor for decades — waiting for someone capable enough to matter.

---

## Escher
**Type:** Vampire Spawn (CR 5)  
**Role:** Strahd's Newest Consort; Former Friend of Doru

### Appearance
Young — early twenties when turned. Beautiful in that vampire-pale way. Dressed in silk that looks like it costs more than most Barovians earn in a year. His anxiety shows in small ways.

### Personality
He is new. He is afraid of being discarded. He was Doru's friend in life — the life Doru tried to save Barovia. Escher chose Strahd instead. He is not sure this was wrong. He is not sure it was right. He is trying not to think about it.

**Voice:** Studied nonchalance over visible nervousness.

**Key Lines:**
- *(at dinner, catching a party member's eye)* "How long do you think you'll last? Not a threat. Genuine curiosity."
- "I made my choice. I'd ask you not to judge choices made under pressure."

### Stat Block Summary
Use standard Vampire Spawn (CR 5).  
Escher will NOT fight the party unless directly attacked.

### Campaign Arc
If Doru was cured (Month 1, Week 2): Escher may reach out via raven late in Act III or IV. He has complicated feelings about Doru's survival. The party can choose to help him seek redemption — or not.

---

## Notes for DM
- **Dinner at Ravenloft** is the primary scene for establishing the brides as individuals.
- Give each bride one memorable moment at dinner — a specific exchange with one party member.
- Their dynamics with each other are as interesting as their dynamics with the party: Ludmilla and Anastrasya tolerate each other; everyone tolerates Volenta; Sasha watches from the margins.
- The traitor Sasha should not be obvious. Let the players be surprised.
