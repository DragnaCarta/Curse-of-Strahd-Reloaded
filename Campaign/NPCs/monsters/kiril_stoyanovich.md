# Kiril Stoyanovich
**Type:** Werewolf Alpha (CR 7, custom)  
**Role:** Brutal Werewolf Pack Alpha; Strahd's Loyal Enforcer; Arc Q Boss  
**First Appears:** Month 3, Week 2 (referenced at the Den of Wolves); Boss Fight: Month 3, Week 4 (Arc Q)

---

## Appearance
Massive. In human form: a barrel-chested man with grey-streaked dark hair, scarred hands, and the posture of someone who has won every fight he's been in. In hybrid/wolf form: even larger, with grey-black fur and amber eyes.

## Personality
He is not complex. He believes power is the only truth, and that the pack that serves Strahd is the pack that survives. He killed Emil Toranescu to take the alpha role. He is brutal toward the pack's weaker members and contemptuous of what he calls "Emil's mercy weakness."

He is loyal to Strahd not from love or fear but from pragmatism taken to its extreme: Strahd is the most powerful thing in the valley, therefore Kiril serves Strahd.

**Voice:** Low, growling, short sentences. He is not eloquent and doesn't try to be.

**Key Lines:**
- "Outsiders. Good. Pack hasn't had fresh kills in weeks."
- "Emil was weak. Weakness gets you dead. I kept the pack alive."
- *(when losing the fight)* "This isn't—" *[hybrid form, no more words]*

---

## Stat Block (CR 7)
**AC:** 12 (natural in humanoid), 13 (hybrid/wolf form)  
**HP:** 144 (17d10 + 51)  
**Speed:** 30 ft. (40 ft. in wolf/hybrid form)

**STR** 22 (+6) | **DEX** 14 (+2) | **CON** 17 (+3) | **INT** 10 (+0) | **WIS** 12 (+1) | **CHA** 8 (-1)

**Damage Immunities:** Bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical weapons that aren't silvered

### Special Abilities
- **Pack Tactics:** Kiril has advantage on attack rolls if at least one of his allies is adjacent to the target.
- **Shapechanger:** Kiril can use his action to polymorph into wolf-human hybrid or a large wolf.
- **Alpha's Howl (Recharge 5-6):** All wolves and werewolves within 60 ft. that are allied with Kiril have advantage on attack rolls and are immune to the frightened condition until the start of Kiril's next turn.

### Actions (Hybrid Form)
- **Multiattack:** Two attacks: one Bite and one Claws.
- **Bite:** Melee +9, 2d6+6 piercing. If target is a humanoid, DC 14 Con or cursed with lycanthropy.
- **Claws:** Melee +9, 2d4+6 slashing.
- **Rend (Recharge 5-6):** Kiril makes a Bite attack. On hit: 4d6+6 piercing and target is knocked prone (DC 17 Str to resist prone).

---

## Campaign Role
- **Arc L (Month 3, Week 2):** He is NOT at the den when the party arrives. He is out on patrol. This is by design — meeting him in the den would be premature.
- **Arc Q (Month 3, Week 4):** Returns after the Ravenloft heist, furious that Emil was freed. Leads the remaining pack against the party in a final confrontation. This is his boss fight.

**After Kiril's defeat:** Emil Toranescu returns as the rightful alpha. The pack pledges neutrality. Several younger members choose to leave the pack and integrate with Krezk.
