# Volenta Popofsky
**Type:** Vampire Spawn / Vampire Bride (Custom CR 6 / CR 9 in Ravenloft)  
**Role:** Strahd's Second Bride; Act II Villain; Ravenloft recurring antagonist  
**First Appears:** Month 2, Week 1 (Coffin-maker's shop, Vallaki)

---

## Appearance
Looks young — mid-twenties at most when turned. Dark hair, wide eyes that blink too rarely. Paint-stained fingers (she makes alchemical weapons as a hobby and art project). She moves like someone who decided gravity is optional.

## Personality
Pure chaotic energy. She is sadistic, but not grim about it — she finds everything delightful, including violence, including her own defeats, including the people who oppose her. She is a hurricane who happens to kill people.

She is inspired by Jinx from *Arcane* and Harley Quinn — chaotic, genuinely funny, genuinely dangerous, and occasionally surprisingly perceptive.

**Voice:** Bright, quick, childlike enthusiasm about terrible things.

**Key Lines:**
- "Visitors! How fun. Henrik never has visitors." *(coffin shop introduction)*
- "Oh! YOU! This is delightful!" *(recognizing the party at Castle Ravenloft)*
- "This isn't over! I'll tell Strahd about you — he'll love you!"
- "You know, for a group of heroes, you're actually quite interesting."

---

## Stat Block — Phase 1 (Coffin-Maker's Shop, Level 4 Party)
*Modified Vampire Spawn*  
**AC:** 15 (natural armor)  
**HP:** 82 (11d8 + 33)  
**Speed:** 30 ft., climb 30 ft.

**Alchemical Vials (Action):** Ranged attack +6, 30 ft. Effect by color:
- **Red:** 2d6 fire + ignites (1d6 ongoing, DC 12 Dex to extinguish)
- **Green:** 2d6 acid + -2 AC until end of next turn
- **Black:** 2d6 necrotic + DC 14 Con or -1 max HP until long rest

**Screech (Bonus Action, 1/day):** DC 13 Con or deafened 1 minute

**Bat Swarm (Reaction, at half HP):** 1d4+1 swarms of bats emerge

**Escape (at 0 HP):** Volenta dissolves into mist and escapes. She cannot be permanently killed outside Castle Ravenloft.

---

## Stat Block — Phase 2 (Castle Ravenloft, Level 7 Party)
*Full Vampire Bride*  
**AC:** 16  
**HP:** 130 (17d8 + 51)

**Multiattack:** Two Claws and one Bite

**Claw:** +9 to hit, 2d6+5 slashing  
**Bite:** +9 to hit, 1d8+5 piercing + 3d6 necrotic  
**Alchemical Arsenal:** As Phase 1, but also includes:
- **Purple (Paralytic):** DC 15 Con or paralyzed until end of next turn
- **White (Flashbang):** DC 13 Dex (area 20-ft cube) or blinded 1 minute

**Frenzied Craft (Legendary Action, 1/round):** Volenta improvises an alchemical bomb from materials on hand — random effect from the color list.

## Campaign Arc
1. **Month 2, Week 1 (Arc D):** Boss of the coffin-maker encounter. Escapes at 0 HP.
2. **Month 3, Week 3 (Arc O):** Present at Dinner at Ravenloft. Manic with glee at seeing the party.
3. **Month 4, Week 4 (Final Battle):** Optional encounter during the heist/final assault. Can be permanently killed inside Ravenloft.
