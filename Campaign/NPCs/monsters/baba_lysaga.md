# Baba Lysaga
**Type:** Archmage Witch (CR 11, custom modifications)  
**Role:** Swamp Guardian; Act IV Major Boss  
**First Appears:** Month 2, Week 4 (Berez — polite first meeting); Boss Fight: Month 4, Week 3

---

## Appearance
Ancient. Impossibly ancient. Centuries of age have given her body a strange, knotted quality — like a tree that has grown in all directions at once. She is small but commands a presence that makes her feel large. Her hut is built from the bones of submerged Berez. Her transportation is a flying skull the size of a horse's head, with room enough to ride.

## Personality
She is completely, gently, sincerely insane. Her delusion: she is Strahd's mother. She was his wet-nurse in life — the obsession calcified over 400 years into genuine maternal conviction. She calls him "my son." She would die for him (and will).

She is not cruel to guests. Hospitality is old magic — she observes it naturally. She is curious, occasionally warm, and will kill anyone who she perceives as threatening Strahd.

**Voice:** Creaking, old, occasionally singing to herself. Interrupts herself with non-sequiturs. Then snaps back to frightening precision.

**Key Lines:**
- *(to party)* "Welcome, welcome. Tea? I have tea. Not for you — I have one cup. Sit, sit."
- "My boy will be pleased when I tell him how I helped. He worries, you know. He pretends he doesn't, but a mother knows."
- *(mid-battle)* "CHILDREN are SO UNGRATEFUL—"
- *(dying)* "MY SON WILL PUNISH YOU! *MOTHER* WILL NOT—"

## Stat Block (Boss Fight, Level 9 Party)
**AC:** 15 (natural)  
**HP:** 114 (12d8 + 60)  
**Speed:** 30 ft.  
*(When riding the skull: fly 50 ft.)*

**Saving Throws:** Int +9, Wis +8, Cha +9  
**Skills:** Arcana +13, History +13, Nature +9, Perception +8  
**Senses:** Truesight 60 ft., passive Perception 18

### Special Abilities
- **Regeneration:** Baba Lysaga regains 10 HP at the start of her turn as long as she is in contact with swamp earth. If the earth is consecrated (Holy Water, radiant magic, or Fane consecration) this is suppressed.
- **Innate Spellcasting (DC 17):** Animate Objects (5/day), Speak with Animals (always), Polymorph (3/day)
- **Strix Command:** As a bonus action, Lysaga can release 3 strix (scarecrow-ravens) from her hut. Each acts on initiative count 20.

### Actions
- **Multiattack:** Two attacks: one Gnarled Staff and one Bewitch.
- **Gnarled Staff:** Melee +9, 2d6+5 bludgeoning + 2d6 psychic.
- **Bewitch:** One target within 60 ft., DC 17 Wis. On failure: target acts randomly (roll 1d4: 1=attacks nearest ally, 2=Dashes away, 3=stands still, 4=falls prone and screams).
- **Treeblight Summon (1/day):** Summons 1d4+1 vine blights.
- **Swamp Grasp (Recharge 5-6):** 20-ft radius centered on Lysaga, DC 16 Str or restrained and take 2d8 bludgeoning per round until freed (DC 16 Str action).

### The Flying Skull (Separate Creature)
**AC:** 12 | **HP:** 80  
**Speed:** 0 ft. | Fly 50 ft.  
**Bite:** +8, 4d10+4 piercing  
If the skull is destroyed: Lysaga falls prone (20 ft. fall, 2d6 damage) and loses her fly speed.

### Legendary Actions (2/round)
- **Move:** Move up to her speed (or skull's speed if mounted).
- **Strix (1 Action):** Release 1 strix as a bonus action.
- **Wail (2 Actions):** All creatures within 30 ft., DC 15 Con or frightened for 1 minute.

## First Meeting vs. Boss Fight
**First Meeting (Month 2, Week 4):** She is helpful — she scries for Ilya Krezkov. She is observably powerful but not hostile. The party should leave understanding they've met something they cannot fight today.

**Boss Fight (Month 4, Week 3):** She knows they're coming. She has prepared the swamp. She fights from the skull, commanding blights, until the skull is taken out from under her.
