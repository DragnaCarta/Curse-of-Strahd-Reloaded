# Morgantha
**Type:** Night Hag (CR 5; CR 9 as part of coven at Old Bonegrinder)  
**Role:** Dream Pastry Merchant; Act II Antagonist  
**First Appears:** Month 1, Week 4 (road to Vallaki)

---

## Appearance (Disguise)
An elderly human woman, wide-brimmed hat, cart full of baked goods. Smells extraordinary — warm spices and sweetness. She looks like someone's grandmother if you don't look too hard.

## Appearance (True Form)
Tall, blue-grey skin, iron teeth, eyes like polished black stones. Claws. An aura of wrongness that makes nearby animals silent.

## Personality
She is charming in a way that's slightly off — like a performance of charm rather than the real thing. She asks questions that feel too pointed for a pastry vendor. She knows things she shouldn't. When her true form emerges, the warmth snaps off instantly — she is predatory, efficient, and ancient.

**Her Business:** Dream pastries trap the eater in a pleasant, narcotic dream-sleep for 8 hours. The dreams are addictive. The children sitting in the mud outside Old Bonegrinder are her regular customers. She is slowly hollowing out Barovia's most desperate residents.

**Voice:** Cracked, warm when disguised. Cold and clipped in true form.

**Key Lines:**
- "Dream pastries! Fresh-baked dream pastries! Sweet sleep in every bite! Two copper pieces, dearies!"
- *(when suspicion detected)* "Sharp eyes, dearie. I wonder what else is sharp about you."
- *(if attacked on the road)* "Not today, not today." *(vanishes)*

---

## Stat Block (Night Hag, individual)
**AC:** 17 (natural)  
**HP:** 112 (15d8 + 45)  
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**Saving Throws:** Con +6, Wis +7  
**Skills:** Deception +8, Insight +7, Perception +7, Stealth +4  
**Damage Immunities:** Fire, Cold  
**Condition Immunities:** Charmed, Exhaustion, Frightened  
**Senses:** Truesight 120 ft., passive Perception 17

### Special Abilities
- **Detect Magic (at-will):** Morgantha can cast Detect Magic at will.
- **Magic Resistance:** Advantage on saving throws against spells and magical effects.
- **Heartstone:** Morgantha carries a heartstone that allows her to become ethereal at will. It is also required for Victor's spirit-mirror ritual (Arc H). She does not surrender it voluntarily.
- **Dream Haunting:** While on the Ethereal Plane, Morgantha can target a sleeping humanoid. The target must succeed on DC 15 Wis or take 3d6 psychic, max HP reduced by same until long rest.

### Actions
- **Claws:** Melee +7, 2d8+4 slashing
- **Change Shape:** Polymorphs into a Small or Medium humanoid. Stats stay the same.
- **Etherealness:** Enters/exits Ethereal Plane.
- **Nightmare Haunting (1/day):** As Dream Haunting but the target also gains a long-term madness.

### Coven Spells (when all three hags present)
The coven can cast: *Eyebite, Hold Monster, Lightning Bolt, Mirage Arcane, Plane Shift, Polymorph, Sleep,* and *Scrying* as shared spellcasting (DC 12, no spell slots, each spell 1/day).

---

## The Heartstone
The heartstone is the key component for Arc H (Victor's spirit-mirror ritual to rescue Stella Wachter). It must be borrowed for 1 hour.

Methods to obtain it:
1. **Combat:** Beat the coven and take it.
2. **Stealth:** DC 18 Sleight of Hand while Morgantha sleeps (she sleeps midday; Truesight still active).
3. **Trade:** She wants something valuable. She will not accept Ireena. She might accept a significant life-debt.

---

## Old Bonegrinder (Her Base)
The windmill on the hill, visible from the road. Three stories, ancient stone and black wood. Morgantha's two sisters — **Bella Sunbane** and **Offalia Wormwiggle** — live here. When all three are present: full coven powers.
