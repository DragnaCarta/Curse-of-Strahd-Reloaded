# Izek Strazni
**Type:** Human (Cursed) Fighter/Warlock (Custom CR 6)  
**Role:** Baron Vallakovich's Enforcer; Act II Villain/Tragic Figure  
**First Appears:** Month 2, Week 1 (Vallaki streets)

---

## Appearance
Enormous. Six feet plus, muscled like a draft horse, wearing splint armor that has seen real use. One arm is entirely normal human flesh. The other is not: a diabolic limb, red-black and scaled, with clawed fingers that curl like a hawk's talons. Fire flickers between the fingers when he's angry — which is often.

## Personality
Simple. Brutal. Loyal to the Baron without question. Not stupid — Izek has a functional tactical mind — but not reflective. He has spent his life in service to simple orders: enforce, punish, protect the Baron.

**The Obsession:** He has been drawing the same face for years. He doesn't know whose it is. He had doll-maker Blinsky create figures of this face. The face is Ireena Kolyana. He doesn't know why. He doesn't know that it's because she is his sister — separated from him in childhood when they were orphaned by a lake accident. His body remembers what his mind has buried.

**Tragic Dimension:** Izek is not redeemable in the traditional sense. He has done too much harm. But the sibling reveal means his cruelty toward Ireena is specifically, heartbreakingly a displacement of love he doesn't have a name for.

**Voice:** Low, flat, direct. Uses as few words as possible. Not because he's trying to be intimidating — that's just how he talks.

**Key Lines:**
- *(on the street)* "Festival. Neyavr 7. You will attend."  
- *(upon first seeing Ireena)* "You." *(just that)*
- *(at low HP, sibling reveal moment)* "...Sister?"

## Stat Block (CR 6)
**AC:** 15 (splint armor)  
**HP:** 112 (15d8 + 45)  
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**STR** 20 (+5) | **DEX** 11 (+0) | **CON** 16 (+3) | **INT** 11 (+0) | **WIS** 11 (+0) | **CHA** 15 (+2)

**Saving Throws:** Str +8, Con +6

### Special Abilities
- **Reckless:** At the start of his turn, Izek can gain advantage on all melee attack rolls through the turn. If he does so, attack rolls against him have advantage until start of his next turn.

### Actions
- **Multiattack:** Three attacks: one Diabolic Arm and two Battleaxe, or three Diabolic Arm.
- **Battleaxe:** Melee +8, 1d8+5 slashing
- **Diabolic Arm:** Melee +8, 1d8+5 bludgeoning + 2d6 fire
- **Fan of Flames (Recharge 5-6):** 30-ft cone, DC 14 Dex, 5d6 fire (half on save)
- **Hellfire Hurl (Bonus Action):** Ranged +7, 60 ft., 3d6 fire

---

## The Sibling Reveal (Mechanics)
**Trigger:** Ireena is present and Izek is reduced to 10 HP or below.

When triggered:
- Izek stops attacking for 1 full round.
- He stares at Ireena.
- If Ireena examines the scar on his collarbone (matching hers from a childhood dog bite): she realizes.
- Izek says "Sister?" and the fire in his arm gutters.
- He will not attack the party if they stop attacking.
- He may die here, or he may be left alive (no longer a threat).

**If left alive after the reveal:** Izek wanders. He is no longer functional as the Baron's enforcer. He is simply a man who has found and lost his sister in the same moment.

---

## Notes for DM
- Play Izek as a genuine threat, not a cartoon bully. He is effective and dangerous.
- The sibling reveal is the campaign's most emotionally complex single moment. Do not rush it.
- If the party kills him without the reveal happening: that is a valid outcome. It's just a sadder one.
- Lady Wachter considers Izek's death a political necessity. She is not wrong. Both can be true.
